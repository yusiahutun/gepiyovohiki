FAQs

1. When was Stade Océane built?

Stade Océane was built in 2012 as part of the preparations for the FIFA Women’s World Cup held in France.

2. What is the seating capacity of Stade Océane?

The stadium has a seating capacity of approximately 25,000, making it one of the largest in the Normandy region.

3. Which football team calls Stade Océane their home stadium?

The Le Havre Athletic Club (Le Havre AC) uses Stade Océane as their home stadium.

4. Has Stade Océane hosted any international matches?

Yes, Stade Océane has hosted numerous international matches, including matches during the Women’s World Cup and various UEFA competitions.

5. Are guided tours available for visitors?

Yes, guided tours are available for visitors who wish to explore the stadium and learn more about its history and facilities.

6. Does Stade Océane offer any facilities for disabled spectators?

Yes, Stade Océane is equipped with facilities to accommodate disabled spectators, including wheelchair-accessible seating and accessible restrooms.

7. Are there any other events held at Stade Océane besides football matches?

Yes, besides football matches, Stade Océane also hosts concerts, cultural events, and other sports competitions.

8. How can I purchase tickets to attend a football match at Stade Océane?

Tickets for football matches can be purchased online through the official website of the Le Havre AC or at designated ticket outlets.

9. Is there parking available at Stade Océane?

Yes, Stade Océane has a dedicated parking area with ample space for cars and motorcycles.

10. Can I bring food and drinks into the stadium?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the stadium, but there are concession stands and food kiosks available for refreshments during events.
