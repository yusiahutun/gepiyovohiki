All Jesse Stone movies in chronological order

Publish date: 2024-09-01

If you are an avid novel reader, particularly crime stories and detective work, then you may have come across Jesse Stone. In his novels, written by the late Robert Parker, he went into the movie world by first starring in 2005, Jesse Stone: Stone Cold, played by Tom Selleck. His sharp, commanding, and reassuring acting skills have made Jesse Stone movies timeless and amongst the most popular.

You may be asking yourself, how many Jesse Stone movies are there? Well, the correct answer to that is nine. The films have crossed over from the previous decades - from 2005 to 2015 ensuring his fans are entertained. Below is a list of all the Jesse Stone movies in chronological order.

Top Jesse Stone movies in chronological order

The correct order to watch Jesse Stone movies is in chronological order. This means, to get a better understanding of the series, it's better to start from Night Passage then Stone Cold. Afterward, you can watch them in their yearly order.

Although the movie was second in production, chronologically, it's the first in the series. Directed by Robert Harmon, the film follows Jesse Stone, a former detective and the new police chief of Paradise. He became an alcoholic after he found out his wife was cheating on him.

His drinking resulted in him getting relieved of his duties. He currently lives with his dog Boomer alone as he is separated from his wife. In his new job, Jesse replaces Lou Carson. On his first assignment, he gets involved in a domestic abuse case, which resulted in him punching Joe Genest, the abusive partner.

Later on, his love for women shows when he starts an affair with Abby Taylor, a local attorney. When the former chief is murdered, Stone tries to solve the case to earn his staff's trust and respect. This 89-minute movie first aired on CBS on 15th January 2006.
