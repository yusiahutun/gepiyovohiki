Karen haircut: What is it and why should you avoid it?

Publish date: 2024-02-15

The Karen haircut might sound familiar to you, especially if you have been keeping tabs on the latest trends on social media. The hairstyle is associated with entitled white middle-aged ladies with racist and overly demanding traits. Usually, they are known for calling 911 and store managers as well as addressing other people aggressively in public places, all for malicious reasons.

At times, someone’s hairstyle talks much about their personality. One of those hairstyles is Karen haircut, which has become popular in most parts of the US. It largely depicts a sense of entitlement among middle-aged white ladies.

What is the story behind the Karen haircut name, though? Nowadays, “Karens” are all over, and not so many people would love to be associated with them. They are hated due to their traits, but they hardly express regrets for their actions. They can go to any length to degrade others because they believe the world exists according to their standards.

What is Karen haircut?

It is simply a short, angled, and layered hairstyle mostly worn by white ladies – inverted bob or lob. It is usually longer in the front and shorter in the back. Though, the style cannot be complete without the chunky highlights.

Initially, it was blonde only. However, with time, the style has evolved – it now includes many colours. Also, others do a combination of colours.

Over time, some people have invented new names for the haircut. For instance, they can also refer to it as “Mom hair” or “Can I speak to your manager?” Well, the history behind these names may baffle you.

Remember, you have the freedom to do whatever you wish with your hair. Thus, learning how to fix a Karen haircut is not a bad thing to do. Most stylists insist that the hair should be short in the back and long in the front. Most ladies like it when the bangs in the front go down to the chin.

Blonde is the most common one but you can also do many colours. The styling of the highlights is also another factor you may want to consider.

Why should you avoid it?

Primarily, the reason why the majority of people hate the haircut is because of the negative tags associated with it. It depicts women who ride on their privileges as a weapon of demeaning others.

Usually, such women will not hesitate to call police on someone. Their malicious motives define their personality. They hardly feel remorseful about their actions. Obviously, they are hated by many because of their entitlement.
