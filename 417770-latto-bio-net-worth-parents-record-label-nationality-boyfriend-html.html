Is Miss Mulatto and Mulatto the same person?

Alyssa Michelle Stephens has used both names. In 2016, she emerged in the music industry as Miss Mulatto in the first season of The Rap Game. She chose the name because, in high school, she was bullied for being biracial. As a result, she opted to settle for the name.

Michelle revealed that as much as the name is controversial, she was passionate about her race. She said:

I’m passionate about my race. I’m Miss Mulatto. The term mulatto technically is a racist slur. It means someone that’s half Black and half white. So it’s, like, controversial... I took that negativity from the word mulatto, and now… Everybody calls me Miss Mulatto.

The term mulatto – Mulato in Spanish – referenced a person of mixed-race ancestry with white European and black African roots.
