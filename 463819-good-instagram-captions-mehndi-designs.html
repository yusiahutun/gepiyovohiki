Why is henna called Mehndi?

The term "Mehndi" is derived from the Sanskrit word "Mendhika," which refers to the henna plant itself. The use of henna for body art has a long history, and the word Mehndi is commonly used in South Asian countries, particularly in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh, to describe the art of applying henna designs on the skin.
