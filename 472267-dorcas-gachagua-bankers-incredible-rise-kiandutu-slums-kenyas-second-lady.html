Dorcas Gachagua: Former Banker's Incredible Rise from Kiandutu Slums to Kenya's Second Lady

Publish date: 2024-09-02
  • Before meeting Rigathi Gachagua, Dorcas Wanjiku had an impoverished childhood following her father’s death
  • Her mother was thrown out by their paternal family, and Dorcas was forced to do menial jobs alongside her seven siblings to survive
  • The mother of two shared how she rose from a house help in Thika’s Kiandutu slums to Kenya’s second lady

Dorcas Wanjiku, the wife of deputy president-elect Rigathi Gachagua, has an incredible grass-to-grace story.

Dorcas, 7 siblings and mother disowned

Dorcas, poised to become Kenya's second lady, had humble beginnings after being born in Ndunyu Chenge in Murang'a.

She spent most of her childhood in Thika's Kindutu slums after her family was disowned by their paternal relatives when her father died.

Dorcas said they were forced to sleep in a chicken coup after their extended family accused them of wanting a share of their grandfather’s wealth.

Citizen TV reports that Dorcas and her siblings were forced to work on coffee farms and do other menial jobs, including househelps to supplement their mother’s income.

Dorcas meets Gachagua

She attended St Francis Girls High School and was accepted into Kenyatta University for a Bachelor of Education degree in History in CRE.

She joined the Agriculture Department of Cooperative bank after graduating.

She worked as a banker until 2006 when she retired and started running the family business and was considering becoming a full-time pastor before her hubby was elected into office.

Looking back on how they met in the 1980s, Dorcas said she almost gave up on life when she got a chance to visit former president Daniel Moi’s Kabarnet Gardens home.

She created a scene while rushing for the podium before Gachagua, an administrator, stopped her.

Dorcas was invited to State House, where she met Gachagua again, and the second meeting culminated in a marriage.

Elsewhere, TUKO.co.ke reported that Terence Creative posed as Gachagua in a hilarious video skit alongside Mammito.
