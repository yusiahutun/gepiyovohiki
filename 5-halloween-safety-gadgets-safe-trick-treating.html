FAQ

Q: What Are the Dangers of Trick-or-Treating?

One of the main dangers of Halloween is motorists.

As children are excited they might step into the road, and without enough light, this could be very dangerous. Using a flashlight or a light-up costume can help ensure your little ones are safe this Halloween.

Q: How Do You Keep Kids Safe From Trick or Treating?

Wearing light-up costumes or reflective clothing is a great way for your child to be seen.

However, you can also invest in extra gadgets like a torch to ensure they can see where they are going.
