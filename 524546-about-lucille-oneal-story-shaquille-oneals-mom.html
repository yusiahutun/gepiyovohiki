Lucille O'Neal's biography

Lucille O'Neal is an American entrepreneur, philanthropist, and author famously known as the mother of former NBA player Shaquille O'Neal. She has gained popularity for her love and support of her son and his siblings.

What is Lucille O'Neal's date of birth? She was born on 20 September 1954 in Dublin, Georgia, USA. Lucille is 69 years old (as of 2023), and her zodiac sign is Virgo.

Lucille O'Neal's parents

According to CBN, Lucille and her siblings were raised by their grandparents after their parents divorced when she was three. During the divorce, their father took them and moved. However, after a few years, Lucille's mother, Odessa, re-entered their lives.

Lucille attended elementary and high school locally. However, she cut her education short after getting pregnant with her first child. Years later, in 1999, she resumed her studies in an Adult Education Program at Bethune-Cookman University.

Career

Lucille O'Neal is an American businessman, author, spokesperson and philanthropist. She and other mothers co-founded the Mothers of Professional Basketball Players Association (MPBP) in 1996. She served as the executive secretary from (1996 to 2000) and as president for some time since 2017.
