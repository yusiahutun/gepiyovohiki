Kakamega postal code: ZIP and complete list of area codes

Publish date: 2024-06-06

Kakamega is known for its unique cultural and ecological significance. The county is home to various tourist attractions, including the Kakamega forest. It is also home to the Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology, But aside from its attractions, what do you know about the Kakamega postal code?

Kakamega County (037) is in the former Western Province of Kenya. Its capital and largest town is Kakamega. The county borders Vihiga County to the South, Siaya County to the West, Bungoma and Trans Nzoia counties to the North and Nandi and Uasin Gishu counties to the East. But do you know the Kakamega postal address?

Kakamega postal code

What is the Kakamega ZIP code? The ZIP code for Kakamega, Kenya, is 50100. The county has a vast mailing system issued by the Kenya Postal Service. This system ensures efficient mail delivery across the county, connecting individuals, businesses, and government institutions.
