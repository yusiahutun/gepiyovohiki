KQ Announces Flight Attendant Jobs for Kenyans with Minimum Qualification of KCSE Certificate

Publish date: 2024-07-27
  • Kenya Airways said it was looking for highly talented individuals to join their flight attendant crew
  • Those interested need to submit their curriculum vitae and other documentation online by February 2, 2024
  • For one to be successful, one must have a high school certificate and have excellent communication skills

Amos Khaemba, a journalist at TUKO.co.ke, brings over three years of experience covering politics and current affairs in Kenya.

Nairobi - Kenya Airways PLC has announced several vacancies, asking qualified Kenyans to apply before the deadline.

In an advertisement on Friday, January 19, the airline said it was seeking to recruit flight attendants to join their team.

What are the requirements for Kenya Airways jobs?

The advertisement said the airline is seeking to recruit highly motivated individuals who can consistently deliver quality service in a safe and secure cabin environment that guarantees guest comfort and satisfaction.

Among the tasks for the flight attendant will include delivering in-flight customer service to set standards for customer satisfaction and loyalty, maintaining proper utilisation of company property to avoid wastage and misuse, and selling duty-free items to maximise profits.

Those to be considered must have a Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) or equivalent and have two years of experience in the hospitality and tourism industry.

How to apply for Kenya Airways jobs

Additionally, interested applicants need to have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, be IT proficient, and have the ability to swim.

If you are interested in applying for this position, you are required to upload your CV and complete the online application on their official website before February 2, 2024.

"If you fit the profile, then apply today. Please submit a copy your detailed Curriculum Vitae here only before February 2. Due to the volume of applications that we receive, only short-listed candidates will be contacted. Kenya Airways is an equal opportunity employer," KQ said.

KQ launches management trainee programme.

Elsewhere, KQ launched its management trainee programme for 2024, inviting ambitious and passionate individuals to apply for the opportunity.

The programme is integral to Kenya Airways' commitment to nurturing talent and fostering innovation.

According to the notice on Facebook, the programme offers participants an opportunity to elevate their careers by gaining hands-on experience, comprehensive training, and exposure across various departments within the airline.

"This programme is designed to provide you with a unique opportunity to develop your skills, gain hands-on experience, and make a significant impact on the future of Kenya Airways and the aviation industry at large," read the notice.
