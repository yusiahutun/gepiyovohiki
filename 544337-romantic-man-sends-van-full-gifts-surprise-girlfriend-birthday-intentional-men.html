Romantic Man Sends Van Full Of Gifts To Surprise Girlfriend on Birthday

Publish date: 2024-05-28
  • A man has celebrated the love of his life in a remarkable way on her birthday, leaving many feeling jealous and inspired
  • The yet-to-be-identified young man ordered a van full of gifts from CBD to be delivered to his girlfriend
  • This thoughtfulness and act of selfless love has been praised by many on social media who admired how intentional the man was

A young man has got ladies online crushing on him and wishing for a fiance like him after demonstrating a remarkable act of love towards his girlfriend on her birthday.

According to narrations in a video making rounds on social media, the young Ghanaian man ordered a van full of flower bouquets and other goodies from Kumasi to surprise his girlfriend, who lived in Koforidua.

The other gifts, as captured in a video on the TikTok page of angiesgiftcode, included a money bouquet, balloons, a cake stashed with money, a blunt cut 22-inch weave hair, a bottle of champagne, as well as many other in boxes.
