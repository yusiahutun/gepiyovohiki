La'Princia Brown is an American woman, blogger, and jewellery enthusiast. She was born on 17 September 1989 in East Weymouth, Massachusetts, United States. Brown is widely known as the daughter of Grammy award-winning R&B singer Bobby Brown.

Who is La Princia Brown's mother?

La'Princial Brown's mother is Kim Ward. She met Bobby Brown when she was 12 and Brown was 11. Kim Ward was born in 1968 in Massachusetts, United States and grew up in Roxbury, Massachusetts.

Who is La'Princia Brown's father?

La Princia Brown's father is Bobby Brown. He was born Robert Barisford Brown Sr. on 5 February 1969in Boston, Massachusetts, United States. He is the son of Carole Elizabeth (mother) and Herbert James Brown (father). He is one of eight children in his family.

Bobby Brown's mother, Carole Elizabeth, was a substitute teacher. His father, Herbert Brown, was a construction worker. They raised him in Roxbury's Orchard Park Projects.

Bobby started performing onstage when he was three years old. He joined the church choir, where he developed his singing abilities. When he turned 12, he started a band called New Edition.

In 1979, Bobby and his band signed a record deal with Streetwise Records and released their debut album, Candy Girl, whose title track was a top-20 hit on Billboard's R&B Singles Chart in 1983.

In 1986, Bobby Brown left his band and moved to Los Angeles to start a solo career. He released his first studio album titled King of Stage. The R&B singer went on to release four more studio albums during his solo career, including:

  • Don't Be Cruel (1988)
  • Bobby (1992)
  • Forever (1997)
  • The Masterpiece (2012)

In 1996, the singer re-joined New Edition and released the album Home Again.

Besides singing, Bobby Brown has appeared in multiple TV shows and movies. These include Panther (1995), A Thin Line Between Love and Hate (1996), Nora's Hair Salon 2: A Cut Above (2008), and Bobby Brown: Every Little Step (2022).
