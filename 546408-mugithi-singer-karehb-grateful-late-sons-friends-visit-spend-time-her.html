Mugithi Singer KarehB Grateful After Late Son's Friends Visit, Spend Time With Her

Publish date: 2024-02-11
  • Mugithi singer KarehB expressed her deep gratitude for the unwavering support she's received from her late son Dulo's friends
  • Despite the profound loss she's endured, KarehB opened up about the heartwarming visits she receives from the young men
  • KarehB painted a picture of her home as a sanctuary filled with the laughter, camaraderie, and shared memories of Dulo

Mugithi singer KarehB, still grieving the loss of her son Joseph Mwadulo, fondly known as Dulo, found solace in the company of his friends who visited her home.

Why did Dulo's friends visit his mum?

KarehB expressed gratitude for the continuous presence of Dulo's friends, who affectionately refer to her as "mum."

In a heartfelt post shared on her Facebook page, the singer noted her home has become a gathering place for Dulo's friends.

"My home is constantly flooded with Dulo's friends, and they all Call me Mum," she wrote.

According to her, the young ones bring with them not only their presence but also motivational talks, hearty laughs, and words of comfort.

The mixture of emotions that fill the air and memories of her beloved son intertwined with the vibrant energy of his friends."It warms my heart," KarehB wrote.

Despite the pain of her loss, she found solace in their presence and the bonds they share.

KareB calls out cons using son's name

In a recent update, the grieving mother took to Facebook to express her distress over the actions of unscrupulous individuals.

According to her, the audacious individuals have been attempting to con her under the pretence of offering condolences.
