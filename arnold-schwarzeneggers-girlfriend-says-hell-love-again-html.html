Arnold Schwarzenegger's first girlfriend says he'll never find love again

'He'll never find love again... he's blown it': Arnie's first girlfriend blasts Terminator star over his womanising ways

EXCLUSIVE By Hugo Daniel for MailOnline
Updated: 21:15 EST, 3 August 2011

Arnold Schwarzenegger’s first girlfriend has written off his chances of ever finding love again days after he celebrated his 64th birthday.

Barbara Outland Baker, who dated The Terminator star for six years, also sent a warning out to other woman he is NOT to be trusted.

Speaking from her home in McKinney, Texas, Barbara, who Arnold also cheated on when he dated her, said: 'I think he’s lost the dearest thing to him which was his family and he may now have blown his last chance of happiness.

Lifting the lid: Arnold Schwarzenegger’s first girlfriend Barbara Outland Baker says Arnie admitted cheating on her during their relationship

Former flame: Pictured here together at his apartment in Santa Monica in 1971

'I’m not saying there aren’t still a line of a million women lining up to have sex with him but I think he’s past the time when he can have a real relationship. No one will trust him.

'This is his rock bottom, maybe he will find his true self now but I don’t have a lot of confidence. I care for him and wish him the best.

'I hope he spends the rest of his life really making up for this. He has been so foolish. '

Buff: The couple had a passionate six-year relationship, seen here on the beach in Malibu in 1972

Barbara dated Arnold from 1969-1975 when he had just moved to California to make himself a superstar bodybuilder and was 'staggered' Arnold cheated on Maria Shriver with their maid Mildred Baena.

She said: 'I was so shocked that he could be so indiscreet to have a relationship with his maid. It was a ludicrous decision, such an immature choice. It’s staggering to me.”

Barbara also feels awful for Maria and the children.

Not changing his ways: Barbara told MailOnline that she believes the Terminator star is unlikely to find love now he's blown it with wife Maria (pictured in 1971)

'It’s the height of betrayal - it’s a double betrayal for Maria that he cheated with a woman she trusted.

'I have deep compassion for her and her kids. They have this sleeve on their bodies now that’s not going to be wearing off. It casts a shadow on their whole past.' 

Barbara, 62, also doubts Mildred was the only woman Arnold cheated with.

'I don’t think Mildred was the only one and she was an unusual choice as well. I think when it’s possible for him to have sex he will have it.'

Animal: Barbara said women would swoon over Arnie and beg him to take his shirt off, pictured during a holiday to Australia with a koala bear in 1973

Barbara, has remained friends with Arnold since they split and wrote a book about their time together called Arnold and Me: In the Shadow of the Austrian Oak - which amazingly the former governor of California wrote the foreword.

Describing Arnold's infidelity, she said:  'Women would come at him, they would feel his chest and beg him to take off his shirt. It was incredible they had no shame.

'When we broke up he finally admitted he had been cheating. We went out to dinner and he said ‘alright I did cheat on you, I felt really guilty at the time’.

Love child: Mildred Baena with her son Joseph, fathered by the actor during his marriage to Maria

'I had an idea, but he always denied it and every time I gave him evidence he would make it seem like I was being stupid.'

Barbara, who is now married to her fourth husband, businessman John, also thinks Arnold's difficult childhood remains a strong factor in his behaviour.

She said: 'He had a terribly dysfunctional family. I think that’s the core of Arnold. He was so dismissed by his father who preferred the older son so Arnold really felt he had to prove himself and had this unconscious drive that most bodybuilders have – this need to prove he’s worthy.'

Barbara revealed it was a randy young Arnold who persuaded her to have sex for the first time – even though she was determined to wait until she was married.

She said: 'I was 21, I had gone a long time saving myself but I was so certain there had never been a love like this on the planet.'

Family: Maria seen over the weekend in Los Angeles with her two sons Patrick, left, and Christopher before having a family meeting with Arnold

Recalling their sex life, Barbara said: ‘He had a really healthy appetite for sex. He was a gentle and very loving. He had a real bawdy sense of humour and was just physical to his core.'

Revelations: Barbara's book

Barbara said she hasn’t spoken to Arnold for three years and she has no idea how the scandal has hurt him.

She is however worried about his health, she said: 'He is still a really heavy cigar smoker, he has that heart valve problem. It has been tough on his body lifting all those weights and his parents both had heart conditions. He has got to take care of himself.'

The blonde also wonders if he ever will change and learn from his mistakes.

She said: ‘We once saw this movie called Love Story together while we were dating and one character says in it "love is never having to say your sorry". Arnold just loved that line. It’s not in his nature to say sorry.'
