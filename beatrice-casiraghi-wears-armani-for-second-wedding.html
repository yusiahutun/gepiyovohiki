Beatrice Casiraghi wears Armani for second wedding

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Picture: Getty Images

Another beautiful wedding celebration meant another dress to match for Beatrice Casiraghi (formerly Borromeo).

Sketch courtesy of Armani

As the newly-minted royal exchanged one more set of "I dos" with Monaco's Pierre Casiraghi (currently seventh in line to take the throne), she slipped into not one, but two more breathtaking bridal looks. 

Getty Images

While she previously wore two Valentino trousseaus for earlier festivities in Monaco, she opted for a different Italian fashion house this time: Armani. Celebrating on the private island of Stresa, Italy, the bride wore a custom-made Giorgio Armani Privé white silk tulle gown with mico-draping, a deep neckline and a pleated train starting from the shoulders. 

Getty Images

For the religious ceremony earlier in the day, the bride opted for another bespoke Armani Privé dress; an ivory-coloured gown with a flared silhouette in Chantilly lace and layers of silk chiffon, paired with a delicate silk tulle veil.
