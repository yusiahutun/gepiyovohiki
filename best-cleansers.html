Best Cleanser | 20 Top Face Washes, Milks, Oils, Balms and Gels

Publish date: 2024-05-18

“I might be the anomaly but I absolutely love washing my face. I thoroughly enjoy the process of massaging my skin, inhaling the scent of the product and I find it satisfying to see the day’s make-up wash down the sink. Because of that, I find myself with a ‘cleanser wardrobe’ which includes Tatcha Cleansing Oil, Muihood Cleansing Balm and Paula’s Choice Calm Cleanser."

"But if I had to pick one favourite, the one I’d repeat buy and recommend to all, it would be Glossier’s Milky Jelly. It works as both a first (just massage onto dry skin and sweep away with a warm muslin cloth) and second cleanse, great for any time of day, and is packed with hydrating and soothing ingredients such as allantoin, pro-vitamin b5, glycerin and panthenol to keep redness at bay. The gentle scent of rosewater is a bonus.”

Medina Azaldin, beauty editor
