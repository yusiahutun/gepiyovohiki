Best facial treatments for brides

Publish date: 2024-04-18

A-list brides have facialist Sarah Chapman on speed dial for a reason. Her massage techniques, high-spec tools and stellar skincare promise healthier, awe-inducing results, so when she recently launched the Wedding Glow treatment, it made perfect sense.

The session starts with a Visia Skin Scanner which allows your therapist to understand your skin's condition in microscopic detail, whether that’s pigmentation, sun damage or fine lines. They’ll then treat your skin accordingly and offer tailored skincare advice for at-home maintenance. We highly recommend booking the package which allows you to have a session six months, two months, and two days before the wedding.
