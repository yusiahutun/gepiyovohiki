    How to Pick the Best Nail Color for Fair Skin?

    a) Undertone- Your undertone plays the most important role in picking the best shade for you. If you have warm undertones, shades like coral, yellow, golden, orange, and warm red, work best for you. People with cool undertones should pick green, blue, teal, silver, and colors having blue undertones.

    b) Finish- The look and finish of your nails depend on personal preference. If you are a party person, shimmery and glittery finishes are what you should eye on. On the other hand, if you want a versatile finish that can go anywhere, matte shades are what you must pick.

    c) Longevity- Longevity can only be promised by gel polishes that last at least 3 weeks. However, they need special equipment to get completely removed. Apart from gel ones, nail polishes that are chip-resistant and smudge-proof ensure better longevity than the others.

    How to Remove Nail Polish?

    To remove the nail polish properly, all you need to do is soak a cotton ball in a nail polish remover and press it on your nail for a few seconds before rubbing it off and completely dissolving the polish. Alcohol works as a solvent helping to break things down in a nail polish. That is why most nail polish removers contain alcohol. When it comes to stubborn gel nail polish, acetone works best to remove it from your nail bed. You can use acetone-soaked cotton balls directly on your nails and cover them with aluminum foils for 10 minutes. With a little effort and a look around the beauty aisles, you can also get the more convenient dip nail polish removers and skin-friendly acetone-free formulations too.

    What nail color is most attractive?

    Nudes and beige usually dominate the beauty industry for being the classiest and most sophisticated nail colors.

    Which are the best nail colors for fair skin tones?

    The best kind of nail colors for fair skin are usually in the shades of cloudy pink and soft blush colors!

    Which nail color makes pale skin look tan?

    Pastels and nudes tend to make pale skin tones look a bit warmer or tanned.

    Nudes and sheer nail colors make your hands look younger.

    What color nails make you look older?
