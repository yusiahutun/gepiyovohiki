Beyonce Shares a TON of Photos from Mom Tina's Wedding -- Blue Ivy Looked Adorable!

Publish date: 2024-09-04

These might be some of the cutest photos ever taken of the Knowles family.

Beyonce just shared a ton of candid pics from her mom Tina Knowles' wedding on her website, showing off her revealing bridesmaid dress, daughter Blue Ivy being adorable and the bride gettin' down at her reception.

This was definitely one helluva party! First, let's talk about that group photo above.

61-year-old Mama Knowles and the entire wedding party were decked out in head-to-toe white, Tina sporting a gorgeous Romona Keveza design with a gold belt.

Meanwhile, Beyonce showed a whole lotta cleavage in her flowy gown, which she paired with a floral headband. Little Blue also wore white, accessorizing with a sparkly tiara.

Also spotted in the pic: Jay Z, Solange Knowles, Kelly Rowland and her newborn son, Tina's new hubby Richard Lawson and his actress daughter Bianca Lawson. Yep, Kendra from "Buffy the Vampire Slayer" is now Beyonce's step-sister!

Daddy Jay Z was photographed dancing with his 3-year-old daughter, who looked like she was having a ball at the reception.

Tina and Richard tied the knot on April 12 during a beautiful ceremony on a yacht in Newport Beach, California. This is the second marriage for Tina, who was married to Beyonce's father Mathew for over three decades, before splitting in 2009.
