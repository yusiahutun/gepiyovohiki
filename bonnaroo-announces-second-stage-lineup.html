Bonnaroo Announces Second Stage Lineup

Publish date: 2024-07-30

By Stab At Sleep from F L O R I D A, United States (Bonnaroo What Stage) [CC BY-SA 2.0 (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/2.0)], via Wikimedia Commons

MANCHESTER, Tenn. (CelebrityAccess) Superfly and A.C. Entertainment have announced the lineup for “The Other” stage for the 2019 Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival, to take place in Manchester, Tenn., June 13-16.

The Thursday, June 13, headliner is Space Jesus, followed each night by RL Grime, Zhu and Illenium.

Others taking the “Other” stage are Sprom, Liquid Stranger, Jade Cicada, Gramatik, Tokimonsta and G Jones, along with Cid, Iglooghost and others.
