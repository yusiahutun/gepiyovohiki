Borman Losing Faith

Publish date: 2024-06-14

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Country performer Faith Hill has left her longtime manager Gary Borman of Borman Entertainment.

"We are so proud of what we have achieved together over the past 16 years," Hill and Borman said in a joint statement. "We love each other like a sister and a brother, but have made the difficult choice to dissolve our professional relationship."

While Hill did not announce her new plans for management, Billboard noted that her husband Tim McGraw recently changed his management as well; dropping Scott Siman at RPM and and signing with Coran Capshaw at Red Light Management.

Borman's roster includes Keith Urban, Lady Antebellum and Jimmy Wayne among others. – CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
