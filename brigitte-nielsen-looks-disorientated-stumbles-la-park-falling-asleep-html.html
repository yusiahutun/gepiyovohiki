The hit films have dried up – and so, it seems, has the glamorous Hollywood lifestyle.

Danish actress, Brigitte Nielsen cut a lonely and worrying figure as she stumbled around an LA park yesterday, sparking fresh concerns over the state of her health.

The 49-year-old former model looked disorientated and appeared to be drunk as she smoked cigarettes and drank from what looked like a vodka bottle in a public park in Studio City.

Time for a nap: After taking a nap in full view of everyone, the actress is thought to have walked back to her home in Hollywood Hills

Dressed casually in trainers, skin-tight blue jeans with a purple sweater and a matching baseball cap covering her shock of bleached blonde hair, the star appeared haggard as she wondered around the park alone, before eventually falling asleep.

The mother-of-four appeared lost in thought as she stared into space, and puffed on numerous cigarettes.

The actress was previously thought to have beaten her well-documented battle with alcohol after she entered rehab in 2007, so these latest pictures must be worrying for her four sons and husband, Mattia Dessi, who is 16 years her junior.

Troubled: The 49-year-old stares into space as appears to be stumbling around as she was snapped wondering around a public park in Studio City in LA


Lost in thought: The former model and actress looked haggard and cut a lonely figure as she puffed on a cigarette

After taking a nap on the grass, Nielsen is thought to have walked back to her home in Hollywood Hills.

The leggy 6'1" actress found fame in the 80s as bombshell, Karla Fry in Beverly Hills Cop II. She then wen on to have a high-profile relationship with Sylvester Stallone but they split just two years later in a very public divorce.

Rocky actor, Stallone's world was recently rocked following the shock death of his son, Sage, 36, who was found dead by his housekeeper at his Los Angeles home.
