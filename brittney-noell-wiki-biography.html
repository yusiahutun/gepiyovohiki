Who is Brittney Noell? Her Wiki-Biography

Publish date: 2024-04-10

Britney Noell is an American entrepreneur. She is popularly recognized for being the wife and life partner of renowned rapper, songwriter, musician, and vocalist, Logic.

She also runs a blog where she posts her personal experiences on recipes, fashion, and emotions. At the same time, she is a rising social media sensation with her Instagram account: @brittneynoellblog, which already contains an increasing number of followers of 35.6K.

Want to know more about the life of Logic’s wife, Brittney Noell? Read this article to learn more about her Wiki-Biography.

How Old is Brittney?

She was born on 1 January 1997 in Los Angeles, USA. As of March 2022, her age is 25 years old.

Wiki-Biography

Brittney Noell is an entrepreneur and a blogger. She is also known as the wife of a famous rapper named Logic.

Talking about her career, she is a designer. The blogger owns the Los Angeles-based clothing brand Brittney Noell Designs and creates her unique design to sell as merchandise to fans and people who have the same taste in fashion as her.

Besides, she is a lifestyle blogger. She shares about her lifestyle, wellness, purchases, motherhood experience, fashion inspirations, and many more through her website. Unfortunately, as mentioned on her Instagram, the website is on hold for her pregnancy break, but soon, she might continue updating it as the baby is out now.

There is only limited information on the web about her parents, childhood, and academics. The blogger is also not fond of exposing her private life to the limelight.

Love Life

Brittney Noell with her family

Currently, the 25 years old lifestyle blogger is a married woman. She is the second wife of Sir Robert Bryson Hall II, known as Logic. After meeting in 2018, the couple took out their marriage license in July 2019. And in September of 2019, they officially announced and exchanged their vows as Husband and Wife.

Valuing their privacy, the husband-wife duo has not revealed much about their wedding to the public. However, different sources indicate that the wedding was an incredibly private occasion.

It was a simple ceremony incorporating friends and family held in the backyard of their house. At present, the pair is parents to a baby boy. They welcomed their firstborn, Bobby, on Feb 6, 2021.
