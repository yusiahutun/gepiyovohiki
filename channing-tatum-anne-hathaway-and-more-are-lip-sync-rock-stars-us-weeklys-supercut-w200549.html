Channing Tatum, Anne Hathaway and More Are Lip Sync Rock Stars: Us Weekly's Supercut

Publish date: 2024-03-31

They must hit up karaoke! Actors are used to entertaining audiences — but impersonating a rock star on stage is a whole other ball game.

In honor of Lip Sync Battle’s return, Us Weekly Video has rounded up some of the greatest celebrity performances. In addition to the Spike series, the Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon has also captured stars showing off their impressive choreography and crazy wardrobe changes. (We’re looking at you, Channing Tatum!)

As previously reported, the Gambit actor took on Beyoncé’s 2011 hit “Run the World (Girls)” during Lip Sync Battle back in December. Queen Bey even shocked the crowd when she showed up for the epic finale.

Other memorable performances on the series include Kaley Cuoco’s rendition of Britney Spears‘ “I’m A Slave 4 U” and Anne Hathaway’s crazy lip sync to Miley Cyrus‘ emotional ballad “Wrecking Ball.”

Fallon, meanwhile, has also invited other stars to belt out a tune — or two, or three! His competition over the years has included Will Ferrell, Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Emma Stone.

Lip Sync Battle airs Thursdays on Spike at 10 p.m. ET.
