Chrissy Teigen's Message from Luna After Breast Implant Removal

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Chrissy Teigen recently decided to get her breast implants removed. And while she recovers from the surgery, her daughter, Luna Simone Stephens, has been on hand with some encouraging messages for her mom.

In a brilliant Instagram post, Teigen revealed that Luna made her a "get well soon" card. "Have fun pulling your boobies out, love Luna," it read. On the other side, Luna wrote, "Bye boobies," which is perhaps the best possible way to show support for Mom's decision. Also included? A mermaid sticker for good measure.

Before the surgery, Teigen took to Instagram to share the reason for her decision to remove her breast implants. The model wrote in part, "I'm getting my boobs out! They've been great to me for many years but I'm just over it. I'd like to be able to zip a dress in my size, lay on my belly with pure comfort! No biggie! So don't worry about me! All good. I'll still have boobs, they'll just be pure fat. Which is all a tit is in the first place. A dumb, miraculous bag of fat. ❤️"

Last weekend, Teigen planned a tea party for her daughter and her friend. "Still planning tomorrow's tea party," she wrote on Instagram. "It's two people. Two four year olds. I'm taking this very seriously and I am def way more excited than them."
