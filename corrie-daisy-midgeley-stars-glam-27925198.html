Corrie Daisy Midgeley star's glam snaps red carpet gowns, tiny bikinis and crop tops

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Coronations Street star Charlotte Jordan, 27, is best known for her role as Daisy Midgeley on the beloved ITV soap.

But it’s fair to say the brunette beauty is a far cry away from her on-screen persona as she regularly enjoys the glamorous lifestyle her TV career brings.

Her character – the TV barmaid – has recently found out that her step mum Jenny drunkenly kissed Stephen at the pub just hours before her boyfriend Leo got down on one knee.

But it’s always a risky game telling Daisy as loyal fans of the show will remember she isn’t particularly good at keeping them quiet.

Charlotte looked positively gorgeous as she turned the star-studded red carpet into her own personal runway when she attended the British Soap Awards back in June.

The brunette beauty rocked a navy-blue floor-length gown for the glamorous event which featured a plunging neckline that finished just above her naval.

Her tulle garment included a layered peplum skirt that cinched in at her waist with a black strappy belt which showcased her beautiful hourglass figure.

She accessorised the killer outfit with a contrasting silver clutch bag and complimentary silver diamond earrings, bracelet and statement hair clips.

The actress showed off her full face of glamorous makeup on Instagram as her loyal legion of fans flooded her with compliments.

This isn’t the first time she has dressed up to the nines for one of her many awards ceremonies, as the soap star shared a snap of herself and her close friend in a jaw-dropping ball gown.

Charlie rocked a ruby red bodycon dress that hugged her enviable curves and showed off her ample assets in a plunging bardot neckline.

She finished the look with a large gold statement necklace and a pair of gold stud earrings as she threw a sultry stare to the camera.

But the actress shared the reality of her wild lifestyle as she wrapped a large fluffy grey dressing gown around her shoulders and completed the look with a pair of black slides.

Sex appeal

All of her red carpet events have clearly spilled into her everyday life as the soap star now boasts quite the stylish wardrobe which she regularly updates her adoring followers with online.

In her latest fashion-forward snap, Charlie rocked a mint green matching co-ord that featured a pair of straight-leg trousers and a matching waistcoat which showcased her golden tan.

Her eye-popping garment featured a plunging neckline as she cheekily unbuttoned the bottom of her jacket to showcase her perfectly toned abs as she posed up a storm in the floor-length mirror selfie.

She accessorised her killer outfit with a simple gold chain necklace and matching gold bangle as she pulled half of her free-flowing brunette locks back into a delicate half-up, half-down hairstyle that she finished with a black bow.

And it appears as though the TV star’s jaw-dropping fashion sense only gets better with time as she took the opportunity to expand her wardrobe with one-off pieces from her travels.

Following her most recent holiday to Corfu last month, Charlotte couldn’t help but show off her summery purchases with her doting fans on social media.

It’s clear to see that the actress can make anything look stylish as she modelled a simple white bandeau crop top and a pair of light wash denim shorts outside a traditional piece of Greek architecture.

She accessorised the effortlessly chic look with a gold pendant necklace and matching gold body bracelet that sat comfortably around her waist.

Bikini bomshell

On her holiday travels, Charlotte sent treated her loyal legion of fans to quite the wakeup call as she sent pulses racing in a stunning mis-matching bikini while she enjoyed a trip out on a lavish boat.

The actress poured her killer curves into a burgundy red bikini top which featured a statement knot in the centre and a bright orange thong bikini bottom.

She showcased her killer curves in the gorgeous two piece which she accessorised with a slew of gold jewellery including a gold bracelet, pendant necklace and a matching gold body chain.

Charlotte finished the look with a pair of tortoiseshell cat eye sunglasses and a lime green bandana to pull her luscious long locks out of her hair.

And this isn’t the first time the brunette belle has rocked bold swimsuit by the pool, as the soap star showed off her endless legs while she posed up a storm beside her hotel pool last year.

The eye-popping one piece featured a plunging neckline and high-waisted leg cut which emphasised her shapely figure as she stretched her legs out in front of her.

For her stunning summer snap, the model embraced her natural beauty as she put on a fresh-faced look as she smiled sweetly to the camera.
