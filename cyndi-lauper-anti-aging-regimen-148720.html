We Can All Keep Having Fun in Our 60s Thanks to Cyndi Laupers Anti-Aging Tips and New Clothing Li

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Cyndi Lauper is clearly a girl who wants to have fun — and always looks good doing so! Thanks to an exclusive interview with Closer Weekly, we can all look and dress like the bubbly songstress — who celebrated her 64th birthday back in June.

“I try and watch what I eat. I exercise frequently — a combination of ballet, light weights, and yoga. And aerobics — because you have to exercise your heart. I think of myself as a professional athlete because I sing, and if my body is an instrument then it better be tuned and doing well so I can perform the way I want to, not just the way I can,” she shared while promoting her new HSN line, Touch of Cyn, which will launch on Tuesday, Dec. 12, 2017 on HSN’s website.

“I go to Tracie Martin quite a bit and use her products. I think they work. They don’t work in time, they work like, you put them on and they make a huge difference. If you get stressed, then it all goes to hell in a handbag! Stress always shows, and it does a lot of stuff to you, so you have to always look better.”

Cyndi — who has been married to David Thornton since 1991 and has a 20-year-old son, Declyn Wallace Thornton Lauper — couldn’t be more excited about this fashion endeavor she chose to embark on. “I got involved with a lot of things, but this was exciting to me because of the affordability for women who normally don’t get a chance to buy the kind of clothes — maybe a more couture style. The quality is nice for that price range. It’s also kind of really targeted to me, towards the invisible woman,” she stated.
