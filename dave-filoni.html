Dave is also well known as, Most famous for his work on the Star Wars: The Clone Wars and Avatar: The Last Airbender animated series, this lead animator also executive produced the Star Wars Rebels series.

Dave is originated from United States. He and screenwriter Bryan Konietzko collaborated on Avatar: The Last Airbender.
