Emma Roberts dazzles in see-through couture on red carpet

Publish date: 2024-07-11

When appearing on the red carpet, one needs to make a statement.

And Emma Roberts did just that as she showed up in a sheer gown that left little to the imagination in New York on Monday.

The 26-year-old dazzled in a Fleur du Mal bodysuit beneath a sheer mustard dress as she arrived at the American Apparel & Footwear Association's (AAFA) 39th annual American Image Awards.

Sheer: Emma Roberts, 26, dazzled in a Fleur du Mal bodysuit beneath a sheer mustard dress at the American Apparel & Footwear Association's (AAFA) 39th Annual American Image Awards

The Scream Queens star showed off her svelte figure in a floral embroidered sheer floor length gown from the Coach Fall 2017 collection.

The sleeves stopped mid forearm and her decolletage was covered in a ruffled manner that matched the hemline.

The AAFA American Image Awards honors those who have exemplified leadership in all sectors of the apparel and footwear industry, including design, manufacturing, and retail. 

Each year, the gala and red carpet event brings together hundreds of influencers including industry executives, designers, members of the media, and celebrities. 

This year's honorees are Andrew Rosen, Founder and CEO of Theory (Person of the Year); Marc Fisher Footwear (Company of the Year); Stuart Vevers of Coach, Inc. (Designer of the Year); Nick Graham (Fashion Maverick); and Ed Nardoza (Lifetime Achievement Award).
