Emmerdale Moira Barton star Natalie J Robb's hottest snaps

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Emmerdale star Natalie J Robb has been gracing screens as Moira Barton for nearly 13 years.

The Scottish star has been at the centre of dozens of high profile storylines since the inception of her character by show bosses from her romance with Cain Dingle to her car crash trauma and steamy affair with young farmhand Alex.

In recent weeks viewers have seen Moira getting to know newcomer Suzy Merton but she could be set to be dealt a blow over the friendship after it emerged that she knew Moira's daughter Holly Barton who tragically died of a heroin overdose in 2016.

In real life Natalie lives a far more glamorous existence than her on-screen alter ago - here we take a look at some of her hottest moments.

Ravishing in red

Natalie wowed attendees at the National Television Awards in this elegant figure-hugging design as she displayed her ample assets with the flattering embellished bardot neckline.

Wearing her hair in a glamorous updo and accessorising with a black clutch bag she was almost unrecognisable as Moira in the waist accentuating design.

The 2017 event turned out to be one of Natalie's most striking red carpet looks through the years.

Leggy beauty

Natalie showed off her fantastic legs as she strolled the red carpet in this cute floral number for The British Soap Awards.

Teaming her dress with a nude clutch and matching nude skyscraper heels she wore her hair down in loose waves and opted for natural-looking makeup for the outing.

The youthful outfit came in 2011 just two years after she joined the cast of Emmerdale.

Green glamour

Natalie went for a more demure design in this green lace and mesh design at the 2018 British Soap Awards.

The actress wore her hair in a tousled pinned up look as she smiled at the cameras with natural toned makeup accentuating her impressive cheekbones.

She held on to a silver embellished clutch bag and wore black nail polish to complete the look.

Stunning silhouette

Natale looked far more Hollywood than Yorkshire when she opted for this stunning form-fitting plunging black gown.

The design clung to her figure in all the right places and caught the eye of all onlookers at the British Soap Awards in 2014.

Her waist was accentuated by the diamante belt buckle embellishment to the midriff of the frock and she accessorised with a simple black clutch bag, wearing her hair in an elegant 1920s style updo and looking tanned with a glamorous makeup look.

Down on the farm

No list about Natalie would be complete without a snap of her in her farming gear as part of her Emmerdale role.

Moira has brought her own special brand of sexy to farm life in the Dales getting viewers hot under the collar with her varying shenanigans along the way.
