My Moms Dermatologist Was in Awe of Her Skin Because of This Cream

Publish date: 2024-07-10

My mom is the most disciplined person I know when it comes to her beauty routine. When she buys new makeup or skincare, she makes a note of when it was bought and when it expires. She’s gone to the same esthetician and dermatologist for nearly a decade and stays up-to-date for her visits to monitor her moles, fine lines, or any other skin imperfections. At her most recent appointment, she got glowing feedback on the lack of lines and wrinkles on her forehead and neck—and it’s all thanks to her recent switch to Eucerin’s Q10 Anti-Wrinkle Face Cream.

The face cream is rich in consistency and is formulated with vitamin E, which is super moisturizing, skin-softening, and an antioxidant that effectively fights environmental factors that can irritate the skin. It also includes coenzyme Q10 that reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. The cream is fragrance-free, great for daily use, and okay for use on more sensitive skin types.

Eucerin Q10 Anti-Wrinkle Face Cream

While my mom loves seeking professional advice from her dermatologist and esthetician, she tries to avoid invasive anti-aging treatments like Botox, so she’s not afraid to roll up her sleeves and test out face creams on the market. One of her friends suggested she give this Eucerin cream a go since they’d seen a major improvement in their skin texture and elasticity. She tested it by using it religiously for three months: once in the morning and again in the evening, every day. 

Within four weeks, she started seeing a difference—and added that a little of the formula goes a long way. She describes it as being thick, but “absorbed quickly into the skin without leaving a greasy film over [her] face and neck,” and that it’s “super hydrating.” Her skin was noticeably more radiant and softer immediately after use. After a month, she no longer experienced dry patches around her mouth, and the stubborn lines on her neck, forehead, and around her eyes were much harder to see. She also said that her makeup “glides on like butter” over it—so much so, that she no longer has to use a primer.
