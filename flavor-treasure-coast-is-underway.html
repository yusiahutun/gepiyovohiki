Flavor Treasure Coast is Underway

Publish date: 2024-06-01

Last year saw the launch of Flavor Martin County, an offshoot of Flavor Palm Beach, where restaurants in the Stuart area offered special prix fixe menus throughout the month of October. This month, the foodie event returns—and it is expanding to include restaurants in St. Lucie County as well as Martin County. “With such an influx of people moving to the area, it was an easy decision,” says CEO Kerri Paizzi of rebranding the October festival Flavor Treasure Coast. “The restaurant scene in St. Lucie County is growing rapidly to accommodate the new residents and will continue to grow. Some fabulous cuisines can be found here.”

Enjoy multicourse prix fixe menus ($35 to $75 per person) at Treasure Coast–area restaurants including Conchy Joe’s Seafood, Dolphin Bar & Shrimp House, Stringers Tavern & Oyster Bar, Stuart Boathouse, Hudsons on the River, Spiro’s Taverna, and many more. See the full list of restaurants and check out their special Flavor menus at flavormartin.com.
