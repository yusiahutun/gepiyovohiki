Florence Pugh hit in the face with flying object by 'shameful' fan at movie panel

Publish date: 2024-02-15

Florence Pugh faced a shocking incident at a movie panel that made the netizens bash the ‘shameful’ fan.

A star-studded cast of Dune Part Two including Zendaya, Timothée Chalamet, Florence Pugh, and Austin Butler joined up at CCXP 2023 in São Paulo, Brazil, on Sunday, December 4. Along with a discussion, the celebrities stood up for a photo op when the bizarre incident occurred.

An unidentified object flies at Florence Pugh during movie panel

Florence Pugh was hit by an object at a movie panel and fans are concerned at the increasing rate of these incidents. The 27-year-old Academy Award nominee was hit while posing alongside Timothée Chalamet and others, who immediately checked to see if she was okay.

Florence handled the situation extremely professionally, managing her composure after mouthing ‘oh, wow’ and looking down to check the object directed towards her.

Fans concerned about ‘disturbing behaviors’

The incidents of popular stars getting hit by objects on stages across the globe are increasing at an alarming rate. British music legend Harry Styles faced something similar at Ernst Happel Stadion in Vienna, Austria in July 2023 when, mid-performance, an unidentified object struck him in the eye.

A month earlier, Bebe Rexha was also hurt on stage as a phone came towards her and she reportedly ended up with a black eye.

Netizens are concerned at the increasing rate of ‘disturbing behaviors’ of fans shown toward celebrities. One posted on X (formerly Twitter): “Why’s everyone throwing stuff at celebrities like, WHY!”

Another echoed the sentiments saying: “Fans’ recent behaviors are disturbing!”

“Is this a new TikTok trend that Gen Z is doing to throw things at celebrities? Seems to be happening a lot recently,” said a third user.
