See Gisele Bndchen's Naked Dress with Visible Underwear

Publish date: 2024-04-14

When it comes to fashion, Gisele Bündchen is winning. The model has donned a variety of looks in recent weeks, from a sleek LBD for her late-night show appearance to a unique take on spring essentials. But her latest outfit was a little less classic and much more revealing.

Bündchen celebrated the launch of her cookbook, Nourish, in her native Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. For the occasion, she posed with her book in a skin-colored, lace cutout midi dress with a bustier detail at the chest. While the color of the dress is a more reserved take on the naked dress trend, the dress gave several peekaboo moments and put the model's underwear on display.

She completed the look with strappy nude heels, oversized hoop earrings, and a bracelet and rings on her right hand. The biggest and best accessory Bündchen wore to the event was an ear-to-ear smile.

"I’m so happy to launch my cookbook, Nourish, in Brazil and have an inspiring conversation about health, food, and well-being with Angélica, Taís e Sílvia," she wrote on Instagram following the event. "Thank you so much girls for being with me on this special day and also thank you to all the other women who were present. I hope you all enjoy the book!"

The model's most recent fashion win came courtesy of a photoshoot with Pop magazine, in which she channeled her inner feline with a leopard printed cutout swimsuit. Bünchen's hair was styled in voluminous waves, and she wore gold metallic heels for a pop of shine to complete the look.

The Nourish author appeared on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon late last month and gave a chic take on the LBD by wearing a black dress with peacoat details and a flared skirt. She completed the look with slouchy knee-high black boots and a pair of black sheer tights.

Nourish formally arrived on bookshelves on March 26, but the model's press tour for the book clearly shows no signs of slowing down.
