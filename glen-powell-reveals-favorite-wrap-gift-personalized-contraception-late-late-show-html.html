Glen Powell reveals his favorite wrap gift of personalized contraception while on The Late Late Show

Publish date: 2024-02-25

Glen Powell shared that his favorite wrap gift of personalized contraception was from Jamie Lee Curtis while appearing Monday on The Late Late Show with James Corden.

The 33-year-old actor said Jamie, 63, gave him contraception with her face on it when they starred in the Fox comedy-horror series Scream Queens together.

'She gave you a condom with her face on it?' James, 43, asked.

Wrap present: Glen Powell shared that his favorite wrap gift of personalized contraception was from Jamie Lee Curtis while appearing Monday on The Late Late Show with James Corden

'One thing she did give me is a not safe for work contraception with her face on it, other thing was the DVD of Perfect,' Glen said. 'She said thanks for the ride of a lifetime.'

James also asked Glen about his recent meeting with the Royal Family.

'I did meet the Royal Family which was actually more anxiety inducing than I kind of expected,' Glen said. 'Have you met any of the Royal Family?'

'Yeah,' James, 43, said.

'There are all these protocols around meeting the Royal Family,' Glen said. 'They would come up to me saying ''Hey when you meet them make sure you say, don't introduce yourself, look straight ahead, do not introduce yourself unless they stick their hand out.'' Don't shake their hand or do any of that stuff. And then they said ''say ma'am jam''. I go ''ma'am jam?' 'And they are like ''No, Ma'am like jam''.'

'The other thing they said is 'to bow from the neck down,' Glen said. 'And then you just start thinking in the mirror, is it, do you move any other part of your body?'

'We actually have very similar protocols here on the show,' James quipped.

James asked Glen about growing up in Texas and about the stories that he was a prankster.

'I've literally outrun the cops,' Glen said. 'That's not a prank that's just illegal.'

'When I was in eighth grade, for example, on the last day of school I released 3,000 crickets in the school hallway right before lunch,' Glen said. 'Ironically my mom was the one who took me to buy the crickets.'

'Why?' James asked.

'Chaos,' Glen said.

'You've been spending too much time with Tom Cruise,' James said.

'My mom literally keeps a fake cockroach on her at all times,' Glen said.

Glen said he actually got his pilot's license after getting a love of aviation from his co-star Tom Cruise, 59, in the sequel Top Gun: Maverick.

Upcoming sequel: Glen, shown in a still from Top Gun: Maverick, said he actually got his pilot's license after getting a love of aviation from Tom Cruise, 59, who stars in the sequel

'He would literally call me after I would do my first cross country flight,' Glen said. 'My first solo flight when I got on the ground. I was signing my pilot's license, this guy, very mission impossible, handed me an envelope and literally said ''please open the envelope.'' In it was a note from Tom that said ''Welcome to the Skies'' and there was a certificate for stunt driving lessons.'
