Haley Denning Career

Before moving to Utah, Denning, an Arcadia High School All-City and All-Region selection, had a career batting average of.588 with 174 hits and 154 runs scored. According to FloSoftball.com, she was the nation’s No. 52 player. She assisted her club team, Firecrackers AZ, achieve a Triple Crown Sports Fireworks Super 32 U16 victory and a runner-up finish in the U18 Super 48.

In 2020, she batted.397, second-best on the squad, with 57 at-bats, 23 hits (second on the team), a team-high 19 runs scored, and the club’s most stolen bases (12-12). She hit.467 and led the team in runs scored with four, while also leading the squad with eight multi-hit games, a.95 fielding percentage, and 18 putouts.

She also led the Utes in batting average (.391), runs (46), triples (5), and multi-hit games (23).
