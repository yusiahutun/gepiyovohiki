Herms Ornaments - Herms H Initiative Ornaments

Publish date: 2024-02-08

In a move that is both sustainable and chic Hermès' petit h initiative—created in 2010 by Pascale Mussard (a 6th generation member of the Hermès family)— utilizes leftover materials of the Hermès manufacturing process and transforms them into utterly witty new pieces like ornaments, display objets and small gifts. The storied French brand calls it a "creative laboratory bringing artists, designers, Hermès materials cutouts and know-how."

This year three petit h shops will pop up around NYC —December 2016 to January 7 at the Madison Avenue boutique, December 10 and 11 at Hermès Wall Street, and from December 16-18 at the Hermès Parfumeri. It's a shot at one-of-a-kind Hermes, or working with an artisan to create your own delightful scarf printed ornament. "Petit h is always about dreams. I tried to make this collection even more festive for the holiday season" Pascale Mussard explains. Because the holidays can always use a little extra color.
