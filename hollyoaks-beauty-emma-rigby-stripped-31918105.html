Hollyoaks beauty Emma Rigby stripped naked for outdoor sex scene in raunchy movie

Publish date: 2024-04-09

Hollyoaks star Emma Rigby once stripped naked for an outdoor sex scene in a raunchy movie.

The 34-year-old actress is set to return to the Channel 4 soap in upcoming scenes 14 years after her character Hannah Ashworth was last seen on screen.

After quitting the serial drama, she started carving out a movie career. Emma stars in 2018 thriller The Protector, which sees her play heiress Camille Logan, whose life is put in danger thanks to a bad business deal negotiated by her father.

As Emma strips off in the film, she cries out in ecstasy in a close up shot at one point. It isn't the only racy scene in the movie as the pair then take their passion to the shower to enjoy another steamy romp. The thriller was distributed by PassionFlix, a streaming service that specialises in adapting romance novels to film, which is based on the book by Jodi Ellen Malpas.

Her role is a world away from her time on Hollyoaks, which she first joined in 2005 aged just 15. One of her biggest storylines involved Hannah battling anorexia, which saw her received a nomination for Best Actress at the 2008 British Soap Awards. She left the show in 2010 and since then, she has starred in films including 2014's Plastic as well as last year's The Festival.

The Protector isn't her first movie for Passionflix though, as she also starred in the raunchy Hollywood Dirt in 2017. More recently Emma starred as Babs in 2021 movie The Power. Hollyoaks spoilers have teased that mum Suzanne Hall is also returning as part of a big new storyline involving Darren, who is the father of her twins.
