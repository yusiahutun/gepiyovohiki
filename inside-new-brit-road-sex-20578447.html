Inside new Brit A-road sex shops as Little Chefs transformed into X-rated stores

Publish date: 2024-03-19

If you’re travelling along a busy A-road, you traditionally don't see many shops – but a new trend of X-rated stores selling sex robots are popping up.

Pulse & Cocktails is a small chain of adult superstores having enormous success with a move into deserted Little Chef restaurants.

The products on offer range from hyper-realistic silicone sex dolls all the way down to impulse items costing under a fiver.

A-roads and sex toys might seem unlikely bedfellows, but Pulse & Cocktails director Graham Kidd spoke to Daily Star Online and explained the thought process behind the unlikely move.
