33 Instant Pot Soup Recipes

Publish date: 2024-04-07

There's nothing like a homemade bowl of slow cooker soup on a cold winter day to cheer you up (and warm you up!). The only way it could be better is to make your meal in half the time—which we can now do thanks to the Instant Pot. Here, we're sharing the very best Instant Pot soup recipes so that you can get supper on the table in less than half the time and have fun while you do it. Instant Pot recipes, after all, are among the easiest, fastest recipes out there, allowing you to whip up quick, easy dinner recipes with a fraction of the effort.

Here, you'll find Instant Pot ideas that you haven't seen anywhere else, from a loaded potato soup topped with cheddar cheese, chopped bacon, and green onions to an anything-but-ordinary Mexican tortilla soup finished off with sharp cheddar cheese, avocado slices, and—of course!—tortilla strips. Even our butternut squash soup has something unique to it: A variety of unexpected spices—including cinnamon, thyme, and rosemary—are included. Each and every recipe on our list features different flavors, textures, and ingredients, to be sure, but they've all got one thing in common: They're perfect for busy weeknights when you're short on time. Delicious, fuss-free family dinners, here we come!
