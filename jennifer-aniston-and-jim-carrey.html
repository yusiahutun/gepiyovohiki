About

Jim Carrey is a 61 year old Canadian Actor. Born James Eugene Redmond Carrey on 17th January, 1962 in Newmarket, Ontario, Canada and educated at Agincourt Collegiate Institute, Scarborough, Canada (dropped out), he is famous for Once Bitten, Ace Ventura in a career that spans 1980–present and 1977–present. His zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Jennifer Aniston is a 54 year old American Actress. Born Jennifer Joanna Aniston on 11th February, 1969 in Sherman Oaks, Los Angeles, California, USA, she is famous for her roles as Tory Reding in the 1993 horror comedy film Leprechaun, Rachel Karen Green in the NBC sitcom Friends, Renee Fitzpatrick in the 1996 romantic comedy film She’s the One, Kate Mosely in the 1997 romantic comedy film Picture Perfect, Nina Borowski in the 1998 romantic comedy-drama film The Object of My Affection, Anne Hughes (voice) in the 1999 animated science fiction drama film The Iron Giant, Justine Last in the comedy-drama film The Good Girl, Brooke Meyers in the 2006 romantic comedy-drama film The Break-Up, Jennifer Grogan in the 2008 comedy-drama film Marley and Me, Beth Murphy in the 2009 romantic comedy-drama film He’s Just Not That Into You, Nicole Hurley in the 2010 action romantic comedy film The Bounty Hunter, Katherine Murphy in the 2011 romantic comedy film Just Go with It, Dr. Julia Harris in the 2011 black comedy film Horrible Bosses and its 2014 sequel film Horrible Bosses 2, Sarah "Rose" O'Reilly in the 2013 crime comedy film We're the Millers, Claire in the 2014 drama film Cake, Rosie Dickson in the 2018 Netflix original coming-of-age comedy-drama film Dumplin', Audrey Spitz in the 2019 Netflix original action comedy mystery film Murder Mystery and its 2023 sequel film Murder Mystery 2, and Alex Levy in the Apple TV+ drama series The Morning Show in a career that spans 1988–present and 1987–present and 1987–present. Her zodiac sign is Aquarius.
