Jennifer Lawrence Looks Angelic in a White One-Shoulder Dress and Chunky Gold Jewelry

Publish date: 2024-04-15

Jennifer Lawrence’s press tour for No Hard Feelings continues—and so does her red-carpet fashion streak.

This Tuesday, the actor attended Sony Pictures’ premiere of the film at AMC Lincoln Square 13 theater in New York City. Lawrence looked angelic in a white one-shoulder dress by Dior, which was cinched at the waist with a thin built-in belt. She accessorized elegantly with strappy golden sandals and gold jewelry: chunky, abstract hoops, a big bracelet with small diamonds, and various rings. For glam, she wore a dark smoky eye and a glossy peach lip. Her hair was parted to one side and styled into sleek waves.

Dia Dipasupil//Getty Images

The appearance came one day after she stepped out in the city for an interview with Watch What Happens Live. For the occasion, the Hunger Games star wore a “ballet-core meets power suit” ensemble composed of a plunging navy blazer with a silky collar and zip-up sleeves, paired over a sheer top with shimmering embellishments. She then added a matching shimmering silver maxi skirt, black sandals, and black square sunglasses.

Prior to traveling back to the city—where she lives with husband Cooke Maroney and their son, Cy—she traveled across Europe for her film. Last week, she attended the premiere of the movie in Berlin, where she wore a striking black blazer minidress. She also attended the premiere in Madrid—wearing a strapless nude dress from Loewe’s fall/winter 2023 ready-to-wear collection, which had a dramatic sweetheart neckline and structured bodice that seemed to float in front of her body. And prior to that, she made an entrance at the premiere in London. For that, the actor looked so elegant in an iteration of a black gown that first appeared in Dior’s fall/winter 2023 ready-to-wear collection—a maxi dress with a sheer bodice and glittering sequins all over. She wore it with black leather opera gloves, classic black pointed-toe pumps, and an array of silver earring cuffs from Anita Ko and Ana Khouri.
