Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck Make Their Grammys Debut

Publish date: 2024-05-15

The Afflecks take the Grammys.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck made their Grammy Awards debut tonight in coordinating glamorous ensembles worthy of two true Hollywood stars.

Getty Images

The "Let's Get Loud" singer looked dazzling as always, this time bringing the shine to the red carpet in a navy and silver crystal-embellished gown by Gucci and matching accessories.

Getty Images

The plunging navy dress was embellished with strands of silver crystals throughout, as well as more strands cascading off the sleeves.

She paired the piece with silver bedazzled platform heels with clear straps from her brand JLO Jennifer Lopez and carried a disco-esque crystal-embellished clutch. She finished the look with stacks of diamonds and silver jewelry: chunky Bulgari necklaces, chandelier earrings, crystal cuffs, a blue-stone silver ring, and her green diamond engagement ring from now-husband Affleck.

Getty Images

Lopez's soft glam (made possible by her own JLo Beauty) complemented her metallic look. The star wore rosy makeup, silver eye shadow, a pink glossy lip, and a baby pink manicure.

Affleck, meanwhile, looked dapper in a classic black suit, matching vest and tie, and crisp white dress shirt.

Lopez and Affleck rekindled their romance in April 2021 and became engaged (for the second time) a year later, in April 2022. Just a few months later, in July, they married in a private Las Vegas ceremony attended only by two of their children, and weeks later, in a lavish wedding at the actor's Georgia estate.

The 2023 Grammy Awards are taking place in Los Angeles's Crypto.com Arena, following last year's event in Las Vegas and its remote setting the year prior at the height of COVID-19. Lopez did not receive any nominations this time around, but that shouldn't—and didn't—stop her from enjoying music's biggest night with her husband.

She shared photos of her full look—as well as close-ups of her shiny accessorizes—on her Instagram. "Hi #Grammys 🤍💙 @recordingacademy #ThisIsMeNow @jlobeauty @chrisappleton1 @robzangardi @marielhaenn @maryphillips @tombachik," she captioned a post, tagging her glam squad.
