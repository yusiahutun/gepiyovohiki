Jim Carrey and His Beard Get Spiritual on Jimmy Kimmel Live

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Here’s a clip from last night’s Jimmy Kimmel Live, where guest Jim Carrey chats with Kimmel about how he feels now that he’s left the spotlight. “Don’t get me wrong – Jim Carrey is a great character, and I was lucky to get the part. But I don’t think of that as me anymore. I used to be a guy who was experiencing the world, and now I feel like the world and the universe experiencing a guy,” Carrey says before pointing out that his beard is now a bigger star than he is: “I always say the question is not ‘Why are you growing a beard?’ The question is ‘Why am I growing a beard and still shaving my balls?’” Check out some more clips from the interview below, where Carrey tells Kimmel about his new Showtime series I’m Dying Up Here about the ‘70s LA standup scene and shows Kimmel some of his paintings:
