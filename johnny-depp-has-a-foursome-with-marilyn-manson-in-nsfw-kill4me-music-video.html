Johnny Depp Has a Foursome with Marilyn Manson In NSFW 'KILL4ME' Music Video

Publish date: 2024-09-27

Marilyn Manson and Johnny Depp just took their bromance to the next level with an all out orgy in the rocker's new music video.

Manson dropped the clip for "KILL4ME" on Tuesday, the second visual the "Pirates of the Caribbean" star has appeared in for Manson following the highly sexualized "Say10" in October.

Directed by Bill Yukich, "KILL4ME" follows Depp as he leers at two women (played by Jocelyn Binder and Bailee Cowperthwaite) and photographs of their semi-naked bodies. The 5-minute video ends with the actor engaged in a threesome with the ladies, before Manson -- sporting a long, blonde wig -- also gets in on the action.

There's not much else to say about this, except there's underwear ripping, butt grabbing and a whole lotta debauchery before the end credits roll.

"KILL4ME" is the latest single from "Heaven Upside Down," Manson's 10th album.
