Katie Beth Hall (born 2004 or 2005) is an American actress who has appeared in television, film, and stage roles. She began her career as a child actor on stage in 2016 and appeared the following year in the TV series Happy! She portrayed young Kim Wexler in two episodes of Better Call Saul and, in 2021, played Sarah Maris in the HBO reboot of Head of the Class.
