Kendall Jenner's Birthday Dress Is Easy Elegance Personified

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Kendall Jenner got a fashionable start to a new year of life.

The supermodel rung in her 28th birthday on November 3 with a low-key celebration hosted by younger sister Kylie Jenner. In photos shared to Instagram, Kendall looked effortlessly elegant in her understated birthday outfit: an ivory off-the-shoulder dress from Khaite’s Spring/Summer 2022 collection.

Khaite Marca Off-Shoulder Pleated Dress

Khaite Marca Off-Shoulder Pleated Dress

The delicate jersey piece features a ruched bodice that extends into a curved basque waistline and a pleated columnar maxi skirt. Jenner accessorized the dress sparingly, simply adding a pair of sculptural silver earrings.

Photos from the festivities show Jenner posing in front of large silver balloons spelling out “SCORPIO BABY.” She also got to blow out candles on four different birthday cakes.

“Twenty ate,” the 818 founder captioned the post.

Kendall has had an eventful, jam-packed year. Other than her typical gigs as one of the most sought after runway models and as the founder of 818 Tequila, she was also recently named the new global ambassador for L’Oréal Paris.

She opened up about her own personal evolution during an interview for Harper’s Bazaar’s September 2023 Icons cover story. “I feel really balanced right now,” she said. “I started my therapy journey a year and a half ago. I meet with my therapist once a week, so every week I’m learning something new. I’m constantly evolving and just excited to do that.”

Jenner added, “My affection for people and my empathy has only grown. My favorite part of getting older is the wisdom and knowledge.”
