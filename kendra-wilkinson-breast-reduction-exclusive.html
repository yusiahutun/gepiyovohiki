I Hate My Big Boobs!

Publish date: 2024-03-17

This is one look Kendra Wilkinson really regrets.

When the "Kendra on Top" star hit the red carpet for the premiere of her series last week, she didn't realize she'd look so top-heavy, with her killer cleavage on full display in a skin-tight, extremely low-cut white jumpsuit.

The look promoted some wild speculation online -- with some suggesting she either got a boob job, or showed off her assets to prove she didn't get a rumored reduction.

"I highly regret that. I hate it. I thought I looked so cute walking out that door," she tells toofab, looking back. "Oh my god, one of the biggest regrets!"

"I love the look, but I don't think that look was made for me. I hate, ugh, I thought it was a cool premiere outfit but it ended up being ... it didn't work for me and I feel miserably," she adds.

The mother-of-two says she's still picking up tips for how to dress for her physique.

"I'm so sad because I should have dressed more appropriate, in stuff that would make me look like me," she continues. "I'm starting to learn this fashion world, I just make mistakes at times, like that outfit."

When asked if this revealing ensemble meant she was more comfortable with her cleavage now, after previously saying she wanted a reduction, her reaction was crystal clear.

"No way! I hate my cleavage, I hate my big boobs," she exclaims. "The whole talk about me downsizing my boobs might come true!"
