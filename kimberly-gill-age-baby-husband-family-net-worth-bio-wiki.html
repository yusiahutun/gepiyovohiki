Know About Kimberly Gill’s Husband And Baby

Kimberly Gill is married to her husband, Jonathan Elliot. The duo engaged in 2016, and after a few months of their engagement, the couple married in June 2016. Their wedding took place in a private ceremony at The Resort Of Pedegral in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico.

Gill and her husband Elliot went to celebrate their honeymoon on Arch of Cabo San Lucas. On 21st June 2016, she shared the picture via Instagram where we can see the newly-wed pair enjoying the boat ride touring a beach.

Kimberly Gill Career And Professional Life

Kimberly Gill worked at WBTW-TV13, CBS as a news anchor and solo anchor from July 2002 – July 2007. She then went to EWS-TV5 ABC in August 2007. Gill worked there for more than four years as a morning news anchor and multimedia journalist.

In October 2011, Kimberly joined at KDKA-TV in Greater Pittsburgh Area and worked as a news anchor. Also, she was a host of news magazine program Your Pittsburgh. As of now, Kimberly Gill is working as News Anchor and Reporter at WDIV-TV in Greater Detroit Area since November 2014.
