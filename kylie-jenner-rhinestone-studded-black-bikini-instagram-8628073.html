Kylie Jenner Wore a Rhinestone-Studded Black Bikini

Publish date: 2024-07-13

It's almost everyone's favorite time of year again: summer. And what better way to ring in the season than with a new swimsuit? Just look at Kylie Jenner, who got a head start on the hot weather by traveling to an undisclosed tropical location, ditching her clothes in favor of an itty-bitty bikini. 

On Saturday, the beauty mogul shared a slideshow of snapshots from her vacation on Instagram, and in the photos, she soaked up the sun in a classic black two-piece with a twist. Her Chanel bikini featured a triangle-shaped string top and matching high-cut bottoms, but both were studded in silver rhinestones for a blingy upgrade. Posing on a rocky cliff while surrounded by palm trees and crashing waves, Jenner further embraced the beach vibes with wet hair, minimal makeup, and no shoes. 

"back in paradise," she captioned her post, which also showed her iridescent manicure and accessories — including, a smattering of rings, silver earrings, and a knitted black beach bag. 

The next morning, Jenner gave her fans an update from her trip with a new batch of photos posted to her grid — this time, looking like a mermaid in a slinky green metallic gown with the sunset in the background. Her maxi-length dress was partially see-through and showed the silhouette of a bikini underneath, while Jenner completed her look with a slicked-back bun, glowing skin, and sunburnt cheeks.

"Twilight," she wrote in the caption.
