Le Creuset Introduced a Pie Bunny

Publish date: 2024-07-15

American bakers have been using ceramic pie birds since the 1930s to prevent their pie fillings from boiling over, but Le Creuset just introduced a thoroughly modern (and adorable) update to these useful steam vents for your Easter pies.

Meet the pie bunny:

Le Creuset

Traditionally these baking tools come in the shape of a bird because of the nursery rhyme ("Four and 20 blackbirds, baked in a pie"), but we're loving this colorful update to the classic.

Le Creuset's pie bunnies come in five colors including Soleil (yellow), Palm (green), Hibiscus (pink), Cashmere (gray) and White.

The pie bunnies are on sale for $12 at lecreuset.com and at Le Creuset's retail stores while supplies last.
