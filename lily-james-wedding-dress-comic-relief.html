You can buy the wedding dress Lily James wore for the Comic Relief special

Publish date: 2024-03-26

If you were blown away by the Comic Relief special which saw the cast of Four Weddings and a Funeral reunite earlier this month, then you might be interested to know that you can actually buy the beautiful wedding gown worn by Lily James in the show. The dress – which was designed by London Fashion Week favourite Ryan Lo – is up for auction now.

In the charity special, James played Miranda (Carrie and Charles' daughter) who got married to Fiona's daughter, played by Alicia Vikander.

While Vikander wore a chic black suit with a blouse, James walked down the aisle in a sleeved, white gown with beautiful embellished rose detail covering it. The dress was designed by Ryan Lo, who revealed the news on Instagram when the show aired.

"Been keeping this national wedding a secret for months," he wrote. "Lily James wears a white rose lace gown by us."

Related Story

And this week, James took to the social media platform to announce that the dress can be yours: "Follow link in my bio to buy my Ryan Lo wedding dress. The dress I married Alicia Vikander in and broke it down with the legends of Four Weddings....trust me it felt good!"

James went on to explain that 100 per cent of the sale will go to Comic Relief and added that the bridesmaid dresses are also up for auction – and there will be a very special addition to the prize.

"This is your chance to win a show stopping designer wedding dress by Ryan Lo and iconic piece of TV memorabilia. And with it, Richard Curtis will give you one page of his original script of Four Weddings and a Funeral - authenticated and signed by him. The bridesmaids' dresses which match it are also available in this Comic Relief eBay auction."
