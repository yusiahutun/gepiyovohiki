Lisa Marie Presley's daughters serve as 'grooms-women' at their father Michael Lockwood's wedding

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Lisa Marie Presley's ex-husband #4 Michael Lockwood invited their daughters Harper Vivienne Ann and Finley Aaron Love to his Santa Barbara wedding to Beauty Beach Lounge hairstylist Stephanie Hobgood on Tuesday.

The 61-year-old guitarist's 14-year-old fraternal twins served as 'grooms-women' alongside his 'best woman' Jeannia Robinetre at the Presidio Chapel located inside El Presidio de Santa Bárbara State Historic Park.
