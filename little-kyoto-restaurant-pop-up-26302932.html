Little Kyoto restaurant pop-up brings taste of Japan to London - and it looks amazing

Publish date: 2024-05-09

Neverland's newest pop up, Little Kyoto, is now open and bringing a glimpse (and taste) of Japan to London.

Whether you visit for a bottomless brunch or cheeky cocktail, or dine in a blossom filled ‘pagoda’ or igloo, the pop-up will certainly keep you satisfied and also give you a lot of Insta-worthy opportunities, thanks to its gorgeous surroundings.

These include the stunning torii archway and the picturesque bonsai Japanese garden towards the Thames river.

Customers can sip on the new exclusive cocktails inspired by Spring, including Yuzu and Lychee Martini, Midori Sour, Raspberry Blossom and Kyoto Mule.

Meanwhile, when it comes to food, you can dine on soft bao buns filled with succulent pork belly, aromatic yakitori sticks, the finest sushi and crispy gyozas.

As well as intimate affairs, the pop-up, based in Fulham, also caters for larger groups with its Ryokan cabins or Sakura lodges.

For those who fancy a night out, resident DJ Harry will be on the decks spinning house tunes to keep the party going along with a line-up of talented live performers, creating a celebration of all things Japanese.

Prices start at £10 for a single table to £120 for a lodge for thirty people.

Little Kyoto is now open for bookings on their website here.

So what are you waiting for? Get booking now to experience a bit of Japan on your doorstep!
