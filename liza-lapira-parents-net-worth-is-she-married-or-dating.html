Get To Know Liza Lapira Parents, Net Worth, Married Or Dating?

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Liza Lapira is an American actress of mostly Filipino descent. Her fans have adored her from the time she played Kianna in the 2008 film 21, special agent Michelle Lee in the CBS police procedural series NCIS, and Ivy, Topher Brink’s assistant in Dollhouse. She has also co-starred in the sitcoms Traffic Light, Super Fun Night, Don’t Trust the B—- in Apartment 23, and 9JKL. Most recently, in late 2022, she landed the role of the leading lady Natalie in the made-for-TV holiday movie, Must Love Christmas.

Now, in the rest of the writing, let us tell you lesser-known stories from her personal life involving her dating life, family background, and more.

Is Liza Lapira Married Or Dating?

Liza Lapira is one of those celebrities who prefer not to discuss their dating life on social media or in interviews. With the rest of her life heavily engrossed in the spotlight, Liza values her privacy when can get it.

So, at this point in her life (in late 2022) Liza did not seem to be dating anyone. She also seemed to have her hands full with more excellent scripts.

Yet, the internet has been aware of the romance rumor that linked her to none other than actor Sam Rockwell.

Sam, who won the 2018 Screen Actors Guild Awards trophy for Best Supporting Actor for his role in Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri, and Liza were reportedly in a relationship at one point. But, unlike Sam’s time together with his now long-time girlfriend Leslie Bibb, the former alleged relationship is not well documented. So clearly, even if there was any truth in Liza and Sam’s speculated romance, they have since long not been a couple.

Since 2007, Sam has been in a serious relationship with actress Leslie. They worked as co-stars in Iron Man 2 and Don Verdean.

Liza, on the other hand, has not mentioned any partners during her long history in Hollywood. Even in recent times, she seemed to be leading the best possible life as a single, yet in the company of her adorable pup.

How Much Is Liza Lapira Net Worth?

Liza Lapira reportedly had an above $2 million net worth as of 2022. After acting on stage and in independent films in the beginning days of her career, she moved to Los Angeles to pursue work in television.

Her first theatrical film appearance was in the romantic drama Autumn in New York. She then went on to do films like The Big Bad Swim, J. J. Abrams’ 2008 film Cloverfield, Table for Three, and 21. She played agent Sophie Trinh in the film Fast & Furious opposite Paul Walker. The next year, in 2010, she played Alva in Repo Men. In 2011, she did the role of Liz, the friend of Emma Stone’s character in Crazy, Stupid, Love. Lately, in 2021, she played Teresa in The Fabulous Filipino Brothers.

In television, she landed her first major role as a series regular on the Showtime TV series Huff. She then secured roles in both seasons of Dollhouse, in Dexter, ER, Monk, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Grey’s Anatomy, Sex and the City, and The Sopranos, and more.

More recently, she joined the recurring cast of Netflix’s Unbelievable in August 2018. In 2020, she was cast as a lead on CBS’ The Equalizer reboot.

Liza has trained under booking coach Amy Lyndon.
