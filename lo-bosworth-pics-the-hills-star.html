Publish date: 2024-07-17

Lo Bosworth is an American former reality TV personality. She rose to fame as a cast member of ‘Laguna Beach: The Real Orange County’ and ‘The Hills.’ The shows documented the lives of her and her friends. The former was a 2004 series that followed her through her high school career with friends Lauren Conrad, Kristin Cavallari, and Stephen Colletti; and the latter was a 2006 spin-off show that followed her young adult life with even more friends, such as Audrina Patridge, Heidi Montag, Spencer Pratt, Whitney Port, Brody Jenner, and Stephanie Pratt. 

Of course, the show also came with plenty of romances, breakups, fights, and just straight up drama. But it all made very good TV!
