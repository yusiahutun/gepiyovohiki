Meaning of Empty Is by Frank Sinatra

Publish date: 2024-08-18

The Frank Sinatra song titled "Empty Is" is an introspective masterpiece that delves into the concept of emptiness and its various manifestations. The lyrics poetically describe the many facets of emptiness, from the sky before the sun rises to the eyes of animals trapped in cages. Each line carries a profound weight, inviting listeners to explore the depths of their own emotions and experiences. Through a seamless combination of spoken word and song, Sinatra captures the essence of emptiness and its impact on both individuals and society as a whole.One standout lyric in the song is "Empty is a string of dirty days, held together by some rain." Here, Sinatra paints a vivid picture of monotonous and dreary days, symbolizing not only a lack of excitement but also the underlying sentiment that life can feel stagnant at times. This theme of stagnation and the cyclical nature of life ties in with the overarching concept of emptiness, suggesting that when one's days begin to blend together, a sense of emptiness can permeate their existence.Another poignant lyric in the song is "Empty is the color of the fear, long about September when the days go marching in a line toward November." Here, Sinatra associates emptiness with the impending change and uncertainty that often accompanies the autumn months. The lyric delves into the complexities of fear and how it can amplify the sensation of emptiness. The imagery evokes feelings of unease, as the transition from summer to fall mirrors the process of grappling with one's inner fears and anxieties.The song also touches upon the emptiness that arises before sleep, with the line "Empty is the hour before sleep chills you every night and pushes you to take me away from every kind of light." This lyric delves into profound themes of loneliness and isolation, highlighting the emptiness that can engulf individuals during their most vulnerable moments. The reference to sleep as a temporary escape from the harsh realities of life further reinforces the pervasive nature of emptiness and the lengths one may go to find solace.Throughout the song, Sinatra's introspective lyrics invite listeners to reflect on their own experiences of emptiness. Each lyric serves as a gateway to a different facet of this complex emotion, highlighting unexpected themes such as stagnation, fear, and loneliness. By intertwining spoken word segments with singing, Sinatra creates a captivating storytelling experience that resonates with individuals on a deep emotional level.In essence, "Empty Is" confronts the universal human experience of emptiness and prompts listeners to explore its various guises. By examining the melancholic beauty captured in Sinatra's lyrics, one can gain a deeper understanding of the profound impact emptiness can have on our lives. The song encourages introspection and provides solace to those who have encountered emptiness in its many forms. Frank Sinatra's masterful rendition of "Empty Is" is a timeless exploration of the human condition and the emotions that shape our existence.
