Meghan Markle Just Re-Wore This Chic Denim Brand Thats on Sale

Publish date: 2024-02-19

Meghan Markle’s fashion reminds me of thank you notes and white wine; it’s classic, timeless, and always exactly what you need. Her Valentine’s Day look didn’t disappoint — in fact, it’s one of her most practical ensembles yet. 

Markle attended the Invictus Games One Year to Go training event in Canada alongside Prince Harry, donning a Victoria Beckham cashmere sweater, Sorel boots, Calvin Klein puffer coat, and Burberry beanie. Though, it was her white jeans that captured my attention — and they’re rumored to be from Frame.

If the chic pants look familiar, it’s because Markle wore a very similar pair — if not the same ones — to the 2023 Invictus Games. While I can’t confirm her exact style, they resemble the brand’s Le High Skinny option, complete with a matching button-front closure, front pockets, ankle length, and ultra-stretchy fit.

Frame Le High Skinny Jean

But I get it — it’s February and winter whites aren’t for everyone. Thankfully, Frame also offers the same silhouette in black, worn-in blue, indigo, and even leather. If you want to save big, consider Frame’s outseam slit version, which features an identical fit but rings in at $83 thanks to extra 30 percent off already on-sale items for Presidents Day. The only problem? The 65 percent off pair is selling out fast. Thankfully, you can still save on the Markle-worn brand by shopping other stellar options. 

Le High Straight Jean

The best-selling Le High Straight jean boasts a cropped, frayed inseam, fitted waist and thighs, and superior stretch, but the booty-lifting back is the cherry on top. The black wash looks elegant with dinner-ready blouses and button-up shirts, as well as both white sneakers and fiery red mules.  Best of all: The jeans ring in at $99 after the double discounts are applied. 

Le Mini Boot Slit Jean

Look to Frame’s Le Mini Boot Slit pants for a vintage-inspired option. The retro jeans include a snug silhouette that contours in all the right places, mid-rise waist, and flared ankle. The smoothing denim makes you appear taller and more put together (pro tip: throw on some platform heels for extra height), while the inner bottom slits provide some peek-a-boo action. 

Whether you have your eye on Markle’s white skinny jeans or are looking for something different, Frame has you covered. Just don’t forget to take a look at the brand’s entire lineup of discounted denim — remember, the brand is offering an additional 30 percent off on all sale items upon checkout.
