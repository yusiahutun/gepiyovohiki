About

Michael Michele is a 57 year old American Actress. Born Michael Michele Williams on 30th August, 1966 in Evansville, Indiana, USA, she is famous for her roles as Selina Thomas in the 1991 action crime drama film New Jack City, Holly Williams in the CBS late-night crime drama series Dangerous Curves, Sandra Gill in the Fox police drama series New York Undercover, Nikki Sheridan in the CBS prime time soap opera Central Park West, Dr. Cleo Finch in the NBC medical drama series ER, Detective Rene Sheppard in the NBC police procedural drama series Homicide: Life on the Street, Veronica Porché Ali in the 2001 biographical drama film Ali, Ayanna Floyd in the Fox prime time musical soap opera drama series Star, Darlene Sutton in the contemporary award-winning Oprah Winfrey Network drama series Queen Sugar, and Dominique Deveraux in the CW drama series Dynasty in a career that spans 1987–present.
