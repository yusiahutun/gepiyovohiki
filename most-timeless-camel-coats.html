The chicest camel coats to invest in for AW23 and beyond

Publish date: 2024-08-20

An outerwear collection is incomplete without an elegant camel coat. 

Hold off on purchasing that trend-focused piece you've been lusting after recently, for when it comes to timelessness, the camel coat wins every single time.

As far as wardrobe heroes go, let it be known: a timeless piece of neutral outerwear is worth its weight in style gold. Navy and black are arguably more versatile, but a well-proportioned, biscuit-hued coat instantly exudes luxury.

Good news for capsule wardrobe fans, the styling options are plentiful. Throw it over a pair of low-waisted boyfriend jeans, use it to elevate a slightly tired neutral polo neck, or layer it over a knitted midaxi and thigh-high boots.

How we chose the pieces:

  • Style: Naturally, a super flattering coat silhouette is essential. When combing through the internet's top fashion sites for the best styles, we made sure that each of our picks had a certain classic appeal for maximum wearability. We've delivered on scope - from Joseph trenches to H&M belted pieces, these are the camel coats we're lusting after this autumn.
  • Colour: The magic of a 'camel' coat lies within the fact that it can be used as an umbrella term for a variety of shades depending on what suits your skin tone. Light tan, latte, dark sand, muted caramel - all these neutrals are united in their semblance to the colour of the much-adored hump-backed mammal's fur. Essentially, 'camel' can be forgiving, and our choices reflect that. 
  • Price: Whether you're planning on splashing out or bagging a bargain, we've got a piece in your price range. We've selected this season's most stylish camel coats, ranging from affordable high street and mid-level brands to luxe designer labels.

Hello! Fashion shares the most stylish camel coats to shop this season: 

  • Wool Maxi City Coat

    Jigsaw

    Cut from a luxe brushed wool, this piece by high street stalwart Jigsaw is great if you're looking to invest, without the designer price tag. The piece features broad lapels and a single asymmetric button for a super flattering line.

  • Mabel Double Breasted Longline Coat

    AllSaints

    AllSaints' Mabel coat presents the sweet spot between tailoring and a relaxed fit. The silhouette is gently oversized, and yet the heavy weight of the blend adds a sense of structure. The finish is brushed rather than fuzzy which makes it feel both smart and classic.

  • Johnson Coat

    Sézane

    Crafted from a wool-polyamide blend, this piece by chic affordable designer label Sézane is the perfect straight-cut, mid-length option. The slim piped pockets don't disrupt the silhouette and the buttons (this is where most high street brands miss the mark) look super luxe. 

  • Emile Wool Belted Blindseam Coat

    Reiss

    Reiss' Emile coat is an excellent alternative to Max Mara's signature belted style. The raised seams add interest, and the tie fastening allows for a bespoke fit. 

  • Responsible Wool Exaggerated Shoulder Overcoat

    Raey

    On the richer end of the camel spectrum, this piece by Raey is categorically cool. Cut from a heavyweight felted wool, it features a super boxy silhouette and an exaggerated shoulder shape.

  • Felted Wool Coat

    H&M

    With a loose fit and a soft shaw collar, this felted wool piece by H&M is great if you're looking to minimise your autumn spend. The belt is detachable - useful for creating different silhouettes depending on what you've layered underneath.

  • Signature Wool-blend Coat

    Totême

    With a soft, draped collar as its focal point, this piece by Totême has a sense of effortlessness about it. Created from a luxurious wool-cashmere blend, it would lend itself to an easy-breezy autumnal layering moment. 

  • Double-breasted Wool and Cashmere-blend Coat

    Loewe

    This piece by Loewe will see you through AW23 and beyond. The classic cut with gentle shaping around the upper half flows down into a cosy ankle-grazing length. With a distinctly tailored feel, the lapels feel sharp and elegant. 

  • Rainwear Chatsworth Belted Double-breasted Shell Trench Coat

    Joseph

    Chic and practical in equal parts, this camel trench by Joseph will fend off the inevitable autumn showers with its water-resistant finish. Crafted from crisp shell, it has a silky, slightly crinkled, effect and poses a rich, interesting alternative to the more classic beige.

  • Textured Wool Blend Coat

    Whistles

    Crafted from a sumptuous wool blend, this piece by Whistles has a slightly hazy, fuzzy texture. Featuring a smart, double-breasted silhouette, it would work beautifully layered over a neutral quarter-zip and cool-girl jeans.
