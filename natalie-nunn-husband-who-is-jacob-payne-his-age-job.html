Zeus Network’s latest original series Baddies South, which follows the successful web show Baddies ATL, which itself is a spin-off of Oxygen’s original TV show Bad Girls Club, premiered on June 12th. One of the stars of Baddies South is Natalie Nunn, who also serves as the show’s executive producer. While Natalie Nunn was romantically involved with many popular celebrities, including Wiz Khalifa and Chris Brown, she ultimately married Arizona Rattler football player, Jacob Payne

Learn everything about Jacob Payne–from his age to his job and love life–in the article below.

Meet Jacob Payne, Natalie Nunn Husband

Jacob Payne, the Arena Football League player, came into the limelight after he started dating Bad Girls Club star Natalie Nunn in October 2011.

Jacob and Natalie announced their engagement on April 23, 2012, and tied the wedding knot in May 2012. Their marriage was aired on October 28th and November 4th episodes of the We TV show Bridezillas.

While everything was going very smooth in the first seven years of their relationship, the couple faced their first setback when Natalie Nunn’s housemate Chloe Ayling spilled the tea to The Sun exclusively about a drunken steamy s*x session with Natalie Nunn and Dan Osborne that happened at a hotel in September 2018 while the trio was filming Celebrity Big Brother UK.

“Dan cheated on Jacqueline with me and Natalie. He had full s*x with Natalie in front of me in the bathroom and then kissed and performed a s*x act on me,” Chloe told The Sun.

After Chloe shared all the minute details from their threesome scandal, Natalie shared a picture of her and Jacob kissing and addressed Chloe’s claims by writing: “PEOPLE WILL SMILE IN YOUR FACE BUT TRULY DEEP DOWN INSIDE CAN’T STAND TO SEE YOU HAPPY! I LOVE U SO MUCH @mrjacobpayne.”

In separate IG stories, she also wrote, “I LOVE YOU @MRJACOBPAYNE.”, “Yall woke the bad girl the f*** up now!”, “On my way to London UK!”

After the incident, The Sun revealed that Jacob was very angry and wanted to dump Natalie. He moved his things out of the couple’s lush home in the US to stay at his friends’ house.

Amid all that, Natalie, however, was determined to save their seven-year-long marriage. In an emotional post, she also shared a picture of them writing: “You’re my best friend! My husband the father of my child! No matter what in this world you sit on the throne you’re my?… This storm too shall pass but in the meantime, I pray we continue to just dance in the rain together ?! I love you @mrjacobpayne.”

Are Natalie Nunn And Jacob Payne Still Married?

As it stands today, Natalie Nunn and Jacob Payne are still married and very much together. It’s safe to say the couple must have figured out a way to make their relationship work despite the explosive threesome scandal.

On their 10th wedding anniversary (May 5, 2022), Jacob shared some pictures of him and his wife and wished her the happiest anniversary. He wrote, “My best friend and my wife for a whole decade now while the future is looking bright‼️Proud of all you have accomplished over the 10 years and the progress and achievements you made…”

How Many Children Do Natalie Nunn And Jacob Payne Have?

Natalie Nunn and her husband Jacob Payne had only one child, a daughter, as of June 2022. Their daughter’s name is Journey Ruth Payne and she was born in April 2017.

Journey turned 5 years old on April 16, 2022; Jacob made a sweet Instagram on his daughter’s birthday.

At such a young age, Journey is the owner of @journeyshair, a one-stop destination for all kid hair care products. Jacob’s daughter has more than 48 thousand followers on her @journey_payne IG account.

The couple had originally announced in November 2014 that they were expecting their first child. However, Natalie revealed that she suffered a miscarriage in February 2015.

Jacob Payne Age

According to The Sun, Jacob Payne was 31 years of age as of this writing.

Jacob Payne Job

Jacob Payne played college football at Central Michigan University. After playing at the college level, Jacob joined Arizona Rattlers in the Arena League, the highest level of indoor American football in America. (Note: Arena Football League is different from the NFL).

After marrying the Bad Girls Club star, Jacob left the Arena Football League to play abroad.

Besides being a football player, Jacob is a musician as well. Some of the singles that he has released in past few years are How Many Times, De Ja Vu, and All In My Head.
