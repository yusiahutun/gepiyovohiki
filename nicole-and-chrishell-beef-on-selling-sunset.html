If you are a fan of the hit Netflix show Selling Sunset, you probably know that there is no love lost between Nicole Young and Chrishell Stause. The two real estate agents have been feuding since season one. But what is the reason behind Nicole And Chrishell beef?

Nicole Young and Chrishell Stause are both real estate agents featured on the popular Netflix show Selling Sunset.

While Chrishell has been a part of the show and a member of the Oppenheim Group, the prominent real estate brokerage, since its first season, Nicole joined the show in its sixth season.

However, Nicole is not new to the Oppenheim Group, as she has been a real estate agent there for a significant period of time.

Nicole And Chrishell Beef On Selling Sunset Explained

Nicole Young and Chrishell Stause found themselves in a heated conflict during the sixth season of Selling Sunset.

In the midst of their onscreen feud, Nicole made an allegation that Chrishell only secured real estate listings due to their boss, Jason Oppenheim, having romantic feelings for her.

Chrishell, who had previously dated Jason for a few months in 2021 after her split from Justin Hartley, vehemently denied the accusation and retaliated by suggesting that Nicole was using drugs, a claim that Nicole denied.

Following the intense confrontation, Nicole opened up about the backlash she faced from the public.

She expressed that although the hateful comments from strangers were hurtful, she found solace and strength in the unwavering support of her loyal friends and family.

Their love and encouragement not only provided her with a sense of upliftment but also reminded her of her true character and identity.

Nicole Young is a highly regarded luxury realtor who has been an integral part of the Oppenheim Group since its inception.

Although she was initially considered to be part of the original lineup for Selling Sunset, she decided not to join at that time.

However, she eventually became a series regular in the show’s sixth season. With her extensive experience, Nicole has represented a wide range of clients, including celebrities and executives.

She has taken on various roles and is known for being a dedicated advocate who fiercely protects her clients’ best interests.

Chrishell Stause, another real estate agent at the Oppenheim Group, made her debut on Selling Sunset in its first season.

Prior to her career in real estate, Chrishell was involved in acting and appeared in popular soap operas like All My Children and Days of Our Lives.

Her transition to the world of real estate brought her to the Oppenheim Group, where she showcased her skills and expertise on the show.
