NikeLab x Olivier Rousteing athleisurewear collection

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Following a sell-out collection with H&M last year, Olivier Rousteing of Balmain has turned his attention to another collaboration, joining forces with NikeLab to create a sportswear line, the NikeLab x Olivier Rousteing: Football Nouveau collection. This is NikeLab's second collaboration as part of their Summer of Sport series.

The designer has taken inspiration from his passion for football and combined it with his power-dressing aesthetic, worn by his #Balmainarmy Kendall Jenner, Gigi Hadid and Rosie Huntington-Whiteley. 

The athleisure wear is every bit as standout as his Balmain designs, an off-pitch uniform that includes jackets and crop tops with metallic gold swooshes, tracksuit bottoms with gold zippers and gold-studded football boots. 

"Fashion is usually about the catwalk and the glamour, but with Nike it was about performance and the athletes," says Rousteing. "What I love about this collection is that we are integrating the iconic style of football into sport style." 

Rousteing has enlisted the footballer Cristiano Ronaldo into his #Balmainarmy for the collection's campaign.
