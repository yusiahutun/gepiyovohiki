Olivia Newton-John and John Travolta just dressed up in their Grease costumes

Publish date: 2024-09-29

Olivia Newton-John and John Travolta dressed up as their famed Grease characters this week for the first time since the film was released 41 years ago.

The actors wore the outfits for a Meet ‘n’ Grease fan singalong event, which involved a screening of the film and a Q&A with the two stars. Newton-John wore a yellow pleated skirt, white shirt and a matching yellow cardigan as part of her 'Good Sandy' alter-ego, while Travolta chose Danny Zuko's signature leather biker jacket and black trousers.

The actors shared a picture of themselves in costume on their respective Instagram feeds. "First time in costume since we made the movie ! So excited!" wrote Newton-John.

In November, Newton-John sold her iconic leather trousers that she wore in the Grease finale for $162,000 (£121,510). The costume was bought by Spanx founder Sara Blakely, who has been a fan of the movie since she was young. The trousers were sold among 500 items belonging to Newton-John as part of an auction raising money for the actress' cancer treatment centre in Sydney.

Earlier this year, Newton-John was diagnosed with breast cancer for the third time.
