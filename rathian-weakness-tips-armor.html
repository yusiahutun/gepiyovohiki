Rathian Ecology
Characteristics
Fire-breathing female wyverns, also known as the "Queens of the Lands." With powerful legs and poison-secreting tails, they hunt mainly on the ground. Sometimes seen preying as a couple, Rathian and Rathalos cooperate well.
