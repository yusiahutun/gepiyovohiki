Rihanna Wore a Silk Trench Coat With Jeans and a T-Shirt

Publish date: 2024-03-08

This goes without saying, but Rihanna has a certain sartorial je ne sais quoi that can make even the most basic outfit look fresh off the runway (see: rain jacket and panta-boots combo and Canadian tuxedo). The singer did it again over the weekend when she turned a graphic T-shirt and slouchy jeans into an OOTD worthy of a night on the town.

On Sunday, the billionaire beauty mogul was spotted solo outside of one of her favorite Los Angeles restaurants, Giorgio Baldi, in a black-and-white tee layered under a Champagne-colored silk trench coat and styled with super baggy oversized ripped light-wash jeans. Sheer bedazzled pointed-toe Gucci pumps peeked out from under the floor-skimming denim, and she accessorized with layers upon layers of pearl necklaces that wrapped around her neck and plunged to below her waist. A diamond wristwatch, a matching ring, a Dior saddle bag, and black rectangular Eclipse-ready Gucci sunglasses completed her look.

RiRi's freshly bleached platinum blonde hair was pulled into a tight updo with Glinda-like curls on top. In terms of her glam, the star opted for bronze eyeshadow and '90s-inspired lined lips. Rihanna first debuted her new icy hue a few weeks ago when she stepped out with a shaggy pixie cut.

This isn't the first time Rihanna has turned jeans into evening wear. In December, the performer attended the launch of her FENTY x PUMA sneaker collab in distressed denim styled with a sporty graphic lace camisole, sneakers from the collection, and a mob-wife-approved purple leather and sherpa hybrid varsity jacket.
