While most people see the beach as somewhere to soak up the sun, play beach games or have a dip, it seems Rihanna has other priorities when she visits a sandy shore.

The Bajan singer donned a white bikini on Wednesday to take part in yet another photo shoot while on a break in Barbados from her Diamonds World Tour.

Splashing around in the crisp, blue water, Rihanna certainly has enough bikini-clad photos to last a lifetime.

The We Found Love singer wore a pair of black RayBan Wayfarers with a flash of red lipstick and styled her shoulder-length black mane into a messy side parting.

She may well have been wearing a lipstick from her VivaGlam MAC collection, which she tweeted about on Tuesday: 'We really worked to make this one of a kind for VivaGlam, right down to the packaging, which has never been done! So shoutout to @MACcosmetics and #ViViaGlam for going all the way with this!! (sic)'.

And after posing for a few snaps the fun began, as she got involved with some watersports along with a few of her homegrown friends.

Rihanna took charge of a jetski and sped across the tropical waters wearing a life vest to keep her safe, but then she got on an inflatable to relax with her mates.

In the water she enjoyed a bottle of beer while her posse enjoyed a few cocktails.

The 25-year-old singer did make sure she had time for her family while back in her home country, as she shared a few snaps of herself and her widowed grandfather, Bravo, as they celebrated his 85th birthday in Barbados over the weekend.

'Threw Bravo a birthday ba$h at mama Fenty crib!! #85 blessed yearz! Many more to you Grandfadda!' the Diamonds hitmaker posted on Tuesday along with a tender image of her kissing his cheek.

'That face... #BravoBirthdayBash,' she added with a studly shot of Bravo.

Winding things down, Rihanna also shared a picture of the two slow dancing before showing her 'gran popz' break it down on the dance floor.

And she is ready to return to Barbados for good after her tour, as she has splashed out $22million on a beachside home on the Caribbean island - after renting the same property last year.

Rihanna bought a home at the luxurious One Sandy Lane resort on the island where she was born and raised.

The superstar always returns to her native Barbados for Christmas and last year MailOnline revealed she rented the same five-bed condo and planned on her mother Monica Braithwaite and younger brother Rajad joining her.
