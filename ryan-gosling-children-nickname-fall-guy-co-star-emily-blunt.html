Ryan Gosling's children have the most adorable nickname for co-star Emily Blunt

Publish date: 2024-08-12

    Ryan Gosling enjoyed a glamorous evening with Emily Blunt on Wednesday, celebrating premiere at UGC Normandie in Paris. 

    As the actor, 43, and his actress co-star, 41, go about promoting their new movie, Ryan has given an insight into the relationship between Emily and his two daughters he shares with long-term partner Eva Mendes, 50. 

    Earlier this week the Hollywood heavyweights appeared on where Ryan opened up about what his daughters Esmeralda, nine, and Amada, eight, think of Emily. 

    "My kids call her Mary Poppins," the star said, referencing Emily's popular role in the 2018 movie . "You're working with Mary Poppins today?' Yes I am," he said, recalling a heartwarming conversation with his little ones.

    The adoration Ryan's daughters have for actress is returned by Emily's own children. The star revealed that her daughters Hazel, nine, and Violet, seven, whom she shares with her actor husband John Krasinski, 44, are huge fans of Ryan.

    Emily revealed that her children are obsessed with Ryan as Ken in the movie released last year. "They love him so deeply. Actually, my oldest one – it's the first movie she has expressed any interest in, and it has nothing to do with me. It’s all about Ryan," the actress confessed.

    "I think it’s Ken really," Ryan quipped, while Emily added: "They were very interested in, like, fascinated [by] what his hair looks like in real life. That was a question I got a lot."

    Ryan keeps his relationship with Eva and their daughters out of the spotlight. Eva starred in the likes of the 2014 American fantasy thriller and the 2012 epic crime movie

    The former model took to Instagram in 2022 to clarify her absence from the Hollywood scene. "I never quit acting, she admitted. "I wanted to be home with my babies and fortunately my other business ventures allowed me to do that more than acting would."

    Speaking to in that same year the star added: "I got tired fighting for the good roles. There just was a point where I thought, 'I'm going to create my own opportunities and become a producer on things and create my own material,’ but it just didn’t feel worth it to me."

    Their daughters are kept out of the limelight but Ryan has opened up throughout his career about his life away from the silver screen.

    "I wasn’t thinking about kids before I met her, but after I met Eva, I realized that I just didn’t want to have kids without her," star told in 2023, adding, "and there were moments on where we were pretending to be a family, and I didn’t really want it to be pretend anymore."
