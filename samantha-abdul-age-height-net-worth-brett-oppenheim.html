Meet Samantha Abdul, Brett Oppenheim Girlfriend

Boss of the Oppenheim Group Brett Oppenheim has a new lady in his life. The Selling Sunset star was spotted while out for dinner on 13 August 2022 with tattoo artist Samantha Abdul in Beverly Hills. The reality star was wearing a button-up dress shirt and dark jeans, and Abdul was wearing a figure-hugging dress with heels and a dazzling purse.

The two met in July 2022 while on vacation in Mykonos, Greece, according to a source close to Abdul, and “they clicked immediately away.” Although Oppenheim and Abdul have been meeting up all over the world, including on a recent trip to Budapest, Hungary, Abdul is based in Hamburg, Germany.

“They have seen each other several times since they met in spite of the big distance,” says the source told PEOPLE. “They enjoy each other’s company and love spending time together.”

Still, the source says that “at the moment they want to take things slow and see where it takes them.”

Abdul already has a child but “has no plans for any further kids of her own,” the source adds, noting this “definitely takes a lot of pressure off the relationship. They can completely concentrate on getting to know each other.” Before meeting Brett, the source claimed that Abdul had been considering relocating to Los Angeles and starting her own tattoo parlor, but now she is “even more determined to take this big step.”

The burgeoning romance comes months after Brett, in answer to a follower’s query, revealed his separation from model Tina Louise in an Instagram story in December. Since April 2021, the former couple has been dating in the open. Shortly after declaring their breakup, Brett posted a homage to his ex-girlfriend on Instagram, celebrating their enduring relationship. He listed the qualities he still adores about Louise in a post that included five images of the former pair.

“Tina is the most genuine, loving, beautiful woman I could have ever dreamed of, with the most breathtaking smile. I will always love her and be her friend,” he wrote alongside the photo gallery. Louise was directly addressed in the caption. “I am grateful for you Tina,” he wrote. “For your love, your laugh, your smile, and for our enduring friendship.”

As for Samantha, she was previously engaged to her baby father Oleg Justus. Oleg is working as a personal trainer at Just.Us.Fitness. They welcomed their son Ilja Nael Justus on 21 January 2018.

In September 2019, Samantha opened up about her engagement and shared, that Oleg proposed to her on 10 September 2017 on the Eiffel Tower. But, the engagement fell through before they could celebrate their second engagement anniversary.

Furthermore, Samantha updated on the 2019 Christmas post that she and her ex-fiance were happy separately.

On Father’s Day 2019, Samantha wrote, “Happy FATHER’S DAY to all the super daddies who are aware of their responsibility and give their little ones love and are there for them, no matter what their relationship with their mother is! You are real men And to all single-power mums who can do it without the guy: HAPPY FATHER ‘S DAY”.
