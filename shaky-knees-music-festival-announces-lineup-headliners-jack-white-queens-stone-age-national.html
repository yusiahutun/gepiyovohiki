Shaky Knees Music Festival Announces Lineup, Headliners

Publish date: 2024-03-01

By Puppysnot (Own work) [CC BY-SA 4.0 (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0)], via Wikimedia Commons

ATLANTA (CelebrityAccess) Shaky Knees Music Festival is expected to return to Altanta’s Central Park May 4-6 with headliners Jack White, Queens Of The Stone Age, and The National.

The sixth edition of the event has more than 60 acts booked for its four stages, including David Byrne, Tenacious D, Fleet Foxes, The War on Drugs, Vance Joy, Cake, Courtney Barnett, Nathaniel Rateliff & The Night Sweats, Lord Huron, Manchester Orchestra, and Matt and Kim.

This year’s Shaky Knees will also see the first festival performance from The Distillers, which is reuniting after 12 years.

General Admission three-day tickets, VIP three-day tickets and Platinum three-day tickets are expected to go on sale today, Jan. 9, at 10 a.m. ET. VIP Tickets include a viewing platform with premium sightlines, complimentary beer and water, a cash liquor bar, appetizers served twice daily, relaxed lounge seating, air conditioned restrooms and more. Platinum Tickets include complimentary beverages, a dedicated entrance, complimentary lockers, and access to the VIP Lounge.

More information is available at www.shakykneesfestival.com/tickets

The festival began in 2013 under the guidance of Masquerade Music Park booking agent Tim Sweetwood, who said that the Atlanta market lacked a festival that booked acts that Shaky Knees now hosts. The festival was originally held at Historic Fourth Ward Park and Masquerade Music Park before shifting to Atlantic Station in 2014, Central Park in 2015, then Centennial Olympic Park 2016-17. Past acts to perform have included The Lumineers, The Strokes, LCD Soundsystem, Cage the Elephant and Pixies.
