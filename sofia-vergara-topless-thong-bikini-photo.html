Sofia Vergara Sunbathes Topless in a Black Thong Bikini

Publish date: 2024-03-10

Sofia Vergara is taking the summer motto “Sun’s out, buns out” seriously.

On Sunday, the Modern Family star shared a rare photo of herself at home—enjoying the sun while lounging nothing but a teeny black thong bikini bottom.

The selfie shows her sunbathing next to what seems to be her at-home pool, surrounded by palm trees and greenery. Her hair is in a natural, straight ’do. She wears mascara and rosy makeup, and is seen sunbathing topless, legs crossed in the air.

In one hand, she holds a compact SPF from her new beauty brand, Toty. The brand—which she recently discussed with Bazaar.com—offers sunscreen, skincare, makeup, and wellness products.

“Lo mio es el verano!😍😍☀️☀️,” she wrote in Spanish in the caption. It translates to “My thing is summer!”

Vergara—who is married to fellow actor Joe Manganiello—does not often post sultry bikini photos on Instagram, though she does show off her style and regularly shares snaps of moments from her daily life.

This past Friday, she posted herself enjoying a girls’ night out. In the pic, she is seen sipping a dry martini while wearing a plunging yellow sundress, diamond jewelry, and dark eye makeup.

And earlier last week, Vergara shared a series of photos from a summer party she had at her home. In front of the same pool deck where she sunbathed this weekend, she posed with her look-alike niece, Claudia Vergara. The two coordinated in white dresses with floral prints. Claudia’s had red roses, while the actress’s had orange roses. They also both wore strappy beige platform heels, rosy makeup, and wavy blowouts.
