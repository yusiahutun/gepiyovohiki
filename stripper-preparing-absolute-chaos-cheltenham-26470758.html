Stripper preparing for absolute chaos at Cheltenham as punters flock to town for races

Publish date: 2024-05-25

A stripper heading to Cheltenham for race week says she is 'preparing for absolute chaos' as punters flock to the races and lap dance clubs for the first time in two years.

The sex worker, who goes by the name of Amélie, is one of dozens of exotic dancers who will be performing in Gloucestershire over the coming days.

Cheltenham Festival has long had a reputation for being the most raucous event in the UK's racing calendar with Brits drawn in for a big night or two after a flutter on the horses.

Controversially for prudish residents of the town, the night life that comes with the races includes pubs and bars transforming into strip clubs.

Amélie who is both a dancer and organiser with Bristol Sex Workers Collective took to Twitter earlier this year asking who will be joining her in Cheltenham.
