Taylor Swift wears $1,800 white and red bomber jacket as she supports boyfriend Travis Kelce on game

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Taylor Swift made a fashionable appearance at boyfriend Travis Kelce's Kansas City Chiefs playoffs game in Buffalo, New York on Sunday.

The 34-year-old songstress — who attended the game with friend Cara Delevingne — braved the cold in a white and red bomber with a hefty price tag.

She donned a varsity jacket from GANT's Kilo Kish Blank Canvas Project collaboration.

The Cruel Summer music artist teamed the look with a ribbed red beanie hat, letting her blonde locks flow out from underneath it.

Her ensemble was rounded out with her go-to matte, bright red lipstick.
