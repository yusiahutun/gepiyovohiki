Vans Warped Tour Line Up

Publish date: 2024-07-24

CARSON, CA (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Vans Warped Tour the annual punk rock extravaganza is almost here. Now in its 16th year, the 43-date tour commences June 25 in Carson, CA and finishes August 15 in Hillsboro, OR. The tour’s kick-off party is confirmed for Friday, April 9 at the Key Club in West Hollywood with performances from a diverse array of Vans Warped Tour 2010 artists including: Andrew W.K., The Reverend Peyton’s Big Damn Band, The Pretty Reckless, Pierce the Veil and Death Punch.


Tickets go on sale Friday. For more information go to warpedtour.com.
