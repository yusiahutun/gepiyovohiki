Who is Angus Cloud's on-off girlfriend Sydney Martin?

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Euphoria star Angus Cloud died age 25 at his family's home in Oakland on July 31, a week after his father's funeral. 

His Euphoria co-stars have led the tributes in the wake of the shock, as has his rumored girlfriend Sydney Martin. 

However, she did not directly refer to Cloud at first, simply posting a black screen with a line broken-hearted  emojis on her Instagram story.

She later posted another tribute, saying 'my heart is so broken. I love you forever,' alongside a video of the pair together.

So, who is Cloud's on-off girlfriend? And how long had they been together? Read below for all you need to know.

Who is Angus Cloud's rumored girlfriend Sydney Martin?

Sydney Martin grew up in Anchorage, Alaska, but moved to Los Angeles aged 18 to pursue an acting career. 

She talked about her difficult childhood in an interview with Voyage LA.

She said: 'My family imploded when I was six and my dad walked out, our house burned down when I was nine, and we lived in our motorhome, homeless, waiting for the rebuild, I struggle with depression and anxiety.'

Now, alongside acting, Martin is mainly a model and has worked for a number of fashion and cosmetic brands.

One of her acting roles so far came as a minor character in Euphoria - Cloud's best-known show - as 'bikini girl' in a season 2 episode. 

She has also starred in All American, Grown-ish, and The Rookie.

How long were Angus Cloud and Sydney Martin together?

Neither half of this rumored couple ever confirmed that they were together, with rumors being that the two were on-and-off since 2022.

However, Martin's emotional response to the actor's death suggest they were together at the time.

She added in her tribute that 'I will never let the world forget you, bless bless one love.'

In addition to adding these messages to her Instagram Story, Martin removed more light-hearted posts made prior to TMZ reporting the news of Cloud's death.

Besides the supposed involvement with Cloud, little is known about Martin's private life.
