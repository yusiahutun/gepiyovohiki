Who is Shaunie O'Neal's husband Keion Henderson?

Publish date: 2024-05-19

BASKETBALL Wives star Shaunie O'Neal tied the knot to her new hubbie on Saturday - but who is the lucky man?

The celebrity bride got hitched at a beach resort wedding to Keion Henderson, a pastor, motivational speaker and singer.

Who is Shaunie O'Neal's husband Keion Henderson?

Shaunie's handsome husband is not only a man of god, but also the CEO and founder of a church community in Houston, Texas, called The Lighthouse Church & Ministries.

Through his spirituality, Keion has inspired thousands through the spoken word worldwide as a talented public speaker.

He's also written a book called The Shift: Courageously Moving From Season to Season that helps readers overcome life's challenges.

The star-studded pastor is decorated with awards for his work, including a CNN Heroes Award nomination and making the The John Maxwell Institute Top 250 Leaders in the nation - a slight change from NBA awards for Shaunie.

Keion's breakthrough album "The River" was even nominated for a Stellar Award, for Traditional CD of the Year, in 2019.

He is originally from Gary, Indiana, and has one beloved daughter from a previous relationship.

When did Shaunie O'Neal and Keion Henderson get married?

The talented pair tied the knot on a tropical island at the Aurora Anguilla Resort and Golf Club on Saturday.

The stunning ceremony was held at the golf club's 10th hole, where nearly 200 guests looked on in joy.

The couple knew they had found their wedding location when they first set eyes on the island and "immediately fell in love with it".

Shaunie told People: "I think after that first visit, we knew this was the place we wanted to get married."

"We wanted our guests to have a weekend of enjoyment. We just like to have fun and love on each other and we wanted to do that with our friends and family."

Shaunie, whose father passed away in February, was given away by her three sons, walking down the aisle to a performance by singer Yolanda Adams.

The breathtaking bride wore a veil, Jimmy Choos and a gown designed by Jean Louis Sabajai and fashion icon, Eric Archibald.

How many children does Shaunie O'Neal have?

The TV star had four children with ex-hubbie basketball player Shaquille O'Neal, but already had a son Myles from a previous relationship.

With Shaquille, Shaunie had Shareef, Amirah, Shaqir and Me'arah.

Their eldest son Shareef, who goes to LSU, is following in his dad's basketball footsteps.

Celeb mum Shaunie described her family as a "package deal" when it comes to relationships.

She previously said: “Keion is an extraordinary man of God. He’s wise, he’s supportive, he’s so loving and attentive.

"He loves me and my children in a way that has changed my life and my heart.”

It was especially moving for Shaunie to have her sons give her away at Saturday's ceremony.
